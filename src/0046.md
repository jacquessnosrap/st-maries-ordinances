# Ordinance 46

An ordinance fixing and establishing a city base, and providing that the official grades and elevations of all streets and avenues shall be fixed and established with reference to and computed therefrom.

BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CHAIRMAN AND BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE VILLAGE OF ST. MARIES, IDAHO:

## Section 1

That a city base be and hereby is fixed and established at a point at the northwest corner of the intersection of First Street and College Avenue, the said point being 2148.30 feet above sea level, and marked by an iron bar.

## Section 2

That all official grades and elevations hereafter fixed and established on any of the Streets and Avenues in the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, shall be with reference to and computed from the city base as fixed and established in Section 1 of this ordinance.

## Section 3

That this ordinance shall take effect and be in force after its passage, approval and publication.

PASSED and APPROVED this 5th day of August, 1907.

Joseph Fisher, Chairman.

Attest:
Thomas C White,
Village Clerk.
