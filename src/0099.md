An ordinance determining and fixing the salaries of the various officers of the Village of St. Maries: 

creating the office of street and water commissioner and defining the duties of that officer; 

repealing Ordinance No. 68 entitled "An ordinance fixing and determining the salaries of certain officers of the Village of St. Maries" approved on the 21st day of June, 1909, 

and all ordinances, parts of ordinances and resolutions in conflict herewith. 

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:

## Section 1

That the village clerk shall receive a monthly salary of $25.00 payable by village warrant at the end of each month. That the Village treasurer shall receive a salary of $50.00 per annum, payable quarterly by village warrant. That the village marshal shall receive a monthly salary of $75.00, payable by village warrant at the end of each month. That the street and water commissioner shall receive a monthly sal- ary of $75.00 payable by village warrant at the end of each month. That the village attorney shall receive a monthly salary of $20.00, payable by village warrant at the end of each month. That the village health officer receive a salary of $50.00 per annum, pay- able quarterly by village warrant.

## Section 2

That the office of street and water commissioner be, and hereby is, created.

## Section 3

That the street and water commis- sioner shall be under and subject to, the direct control of the board of trustees, and he shall have, in addition to any general or special duties that the board of trustees may from time to time cause to be devolved upon that officer, the fol- lowing duties, to-wit: General supervision of the streets, highways and alleys of the village. General supervision and control of the water system of the village. To make, or cause to be made, all needful repairs and improvements to the water system of the village, and to make necessary taps and connections thereto, subject at all times to the directions of the board of trustees. To make a monthly report to the board of trustees to be oral or written and of such nature and kind as the board of trustees in the exercise of their discretion may determine and which re- port is to be presented to the board of trustees at their regular monthly meeting. Such report should show the general condition of the streets, alleys and highways of the village, and be ac- companied by the recommendation of the street and water commissioner, and should also show the general condition of the water system of the village accompanied by suggestions relating to that system.

## Section 4

That ordinance No. 68 entitled "An ordinance fixing and determining the salar- ies of certain village officers of the Village of St. Maries," approved on the 21st day of June, 1909, and all ordinances, parts of ordinances and reso- lutions in conflict herewith be, and hereby are, repealed.

## Section 5

That this ordinance shall be effec- tive from and after its passage, approval and publication.

Approved this 16th day of June, A. D., 1911.
O. E. HAILEY
Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the vil- lage of St. Maries.
Attest:
CHAS. R. SCHULTE,
Clerk.
