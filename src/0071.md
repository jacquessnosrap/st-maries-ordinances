# Ordinance 71

An ordinance to vacate a portion of Highland Avenue within the Village St. Maries, Idaho. *And preserving right of way over said  . . . so vacated for the propose of water case a where pope i so as avenue.*[^1]

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of Maries:

## Section 1
That all that certain portion of Highland Avenue Lying and being East of the Alley in Block One (1) of Martins Addition to the Village of St. Maries and extending from said alley to the West line of Second Street be and the same is hereby vacated, and annulled.

## Section 2 
That said portion of said Avenue is vacated upon the agreement by and between the property holders abutting said Avenue and the Board of Trustees of said Village that said Village shall have the right to maintain it water pipe laid in said Avenue with full right of way over said Avenue to haul supplies to renew or repair same and with full right and power to dig up said pipe and repair or relay the same.

## Section 3 
That this ordinanes shall take effect from and after its passage, approval and publication in one issue of the St. Maries Gazette a weekly newspaper published in said Village.

Approved this 26 day of June A.D. 1909
Perley Markin
Chairman of Said Board.

Attest.

J. S. Turnbull
Village Clerk.


[^1]: The portion of the orginal text that is represented by the italicized text is a hand-written emendation and is uncertain in its transcription.
