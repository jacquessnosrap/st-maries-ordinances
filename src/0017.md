# Ordinance 17

An Ordinance providing for the taxation of dogs.

Be it ordained by the chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries That Section 1,of Ordinance No 7 be and is hereby amended and shall read as follows.

That the owners or persons having in charge any dog permitted to run at large within the corporate limits of this Village shall pay into the Village Treasure, as a License the sum of three Dollars for each male dog, anthe sum of Five Dollars, for each female dog which shall entitle him to a receipt from the Marshall designating the name of the owner and the number of the License which number shall correspond with that worn by the dog. This Ordinance to be in force on and after its approval and Publication.

All Ordinances or parts of Ordinances in conflict with this Ordinance are hereby repealed.

Jan 2 1903

George Hawk
Clerk.

J W Skelton
Chair,
