An Ordinance creating the local Sewerage Improvement District No Five int eh City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho. 

Defining the Boundaries Thereof, and Providing for the Construction of a Sewerag System Thereing;

Providing for the Apointment of a Sewer Committee and Defining and Regulating their Powers and Durties;

Providing for the Levying of a Special Assessment for the Sewerage Improvement and the Expenses Connected Therewith and Providing for the Issuing of Bonds Therefore;

Providing for the Payment of said Asessment in Ten (10) Equal Annual installments and for the Collection Thereof;

Providing for the Redemption of Said Bonds;
