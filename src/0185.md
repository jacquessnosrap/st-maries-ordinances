AN ORDINANCE AMENDING SECTIONS 1, 2 and 3 OF ORDINANCE NO. 183, BEING AN ORDINANCE PRESCRIBING CERTAIN RULES AND REGULATIONS FOR THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE WATER DEPARTMENT OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES. 

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:--

## Section 1

That Section 1 of Ordinance No. 183, being an ordinance prescribing certain rules and regulations for the administration of the water department of the City of St. Maries, be amended to read as follows:

### Section 1 

That immediately upon the taking effect of this ordinance, all water consumers in the city of St. Maries taking water from the water department of said city, shall be placed upon a meter basis and be required to install water meters in or upon their premises as a condition prece- dent for the use or purchase of water from the water depart- ment.

That immediately upon the taking effect of this ordinance, the water department shall proceed to install, upon the premises of all water users who may desire the same to be done, water maters, which meters so installed by the city shall be purchased in the first instance, and the expense of installation thereof paid in the first instance by the city of St. Maries.

That the cost of said meters and of the installation thereof, when purchased and installed by the city, shall be chargeable to each of said property owners at the rate of Nine Dollars cash when installed; or at the rate of Ten Dollars each pyable on time at the rate of Fifty Cents per month until the entire sum of Ten Dollars shall be paid. That in case any present consumer of water does not, upon the taking effect of this ordinance, proceed to install a water meter in or upon his premises in accordance with the provisions hereof, or permit the city of St. Maries to install a meter thereon, said consumer shall be refused the purchase of water from the city.

That hereafter, when application shall be made to the water department of the city of St. Maries for the use of water, the applicant shall install a water meter upon his premises, or permit the water department to install the same, under the same conditions as aforesaid before such applicant shall be furnished with water and the cost of such meter and the cost of the installation thereof shall be the same as stated in the preceding paragraph.

When the water consumer pays for said meters installed by the city on time at the price of ten dollars each, and at the rate of fifty cents per month, such monthly instalments of fifty cents each shall be added to the regular monthly water bill of such consumer, or in case there may be no water charge against mer for particular month, such instalments of cents shall con constitute such cont consumer's water charge for such month; provided, however, that in the event that said monthly instalment of fifty cents, or any part there- of shall remain unpaid on the 10th day of each and every month, the same will become delinquent, and if still delinquent on the 15th day of the month, the same penalty will attach to said water consumer and his premises as shall attach in case of a non- payment of the water charge for such premises, and in addition thereto the water department of the city of St. Maries shall have the right to enter upon said premises and remove said water meter therefrom, and all payments made upon said water meter shall be forfeited and the said city shall have the right to retain the payments made, for and in consideration of the rent for such water meter during the time that the same was in use.

Such water meters so installed by the city shall be and remain the property of the city of St. Maries until fully paid for by the purchaser; but upon the payment by such purchaser or water consumer of the full sum of Nine Dollars ($9) cash, or of Ten Dollars ($10) when paid on time, each water meter so paid for shall become the property of such water consumer or purchaser.

### Section 2

Pipes or hose to be used only in case of fire in build- ings or premises, will be attached to buildings or on premises without charge for water used for fire purposes on the condi- tions that the fire pipes be disconnected from all others, and hose, pipes or branches must be sealed, to be broken only in case of fire.

## Section 3 That Section 3 of said Ordinance No. 183 be amded to read as follows, to-wit:--

### Section 3

That hereafter all the water purchased from the water department of the city of St. Maries shall be measured and paid for according to meter measurements. And each consumer of water shall, or permit the water department to, immediately after the taking effect of this ordinance, pro- ceed to install water meters upon his, her or its premises according to the provisions of this ordinance, such installa- tion to be made as soon as reasonably practicable.

Until such time as water meters shall have been in- stalled upon the premises of all water consumers in the city of St. Maries, the water consumers upon whose premises water meters have not yet been installed shall pay for the water used by them according to the flat rate now charged water con- sumers under the provisions of Ordinance No. 79, and during the period which the water department may be engaged in the process of installing meters throughout the city, the provisions of Ordinance No. 79 shall remain in full force and effect as regards such water consumers upon whose premises water meters have not been installed.
