AN ORDINANCE PROHIBITING THE DRAGGING OF POLES, LOGS, TIES, DRAG RACKS, LOGGING DRAYS, AND LIKE VEHICLES UPON GRADED AND OTHERWISE IMPROVED STREETS OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES:

PROHIBITING THE BACKING OF VEHICLES INTO ANY OF THE CURBS ON ANY OF THE STREETS OF ST, MARIES AND 

PROHIBITING ANY VEHICLE FROM BEING DRIVEN OR OTHERWISE PROPELLED ACROSS OR UPON ANY CURB OR ACROSS OR UPON ANY GUTTER WITHIN THE CITY OF ST, MARIES OTHER THAN AT THE PLACES PROVIDED FOR SUCH GROSSING:

MAKING ANY OF SAID ACTS A MISDEANOR AND PROVIDING A PENALTY THERE FOR AND

REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and Common Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:-

## Section 1.

That it be and hereby is, prohibited and made unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation, to drag, push, or otherwise propell any pole, log, tie, board, or other piece of timber over any graded or otherwise improved street within the City of St. Maries, Idaho, or cuase the same to be done.

## Section 2.

That it be and hereby is prohibited and made unlawful, for any person, firm, or corporation, to drive, drag, nush, pull, or otherwise propell, over or upon any graded or otherwise improved street within the City of St. Maries, Idaho, amy drag rack, logging dray, like or any vehicle or thing which thereby is destructive or tends to be destructive to the surface, base, or any part of such street.

## Section 3.

That it be and hereby is prohibited and made unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation, to back, push, or otherwise propel any vehicle of any nature whatsoever, into any curb on any streets of the City of St. Maries, or cause the same to be done.

## Section 4.

That it be, and hereby is, prohibited and made unlawful for any person, firm, or dorporation, to drive, drag, mush or pull, or otherwise propel across or upon any curb or scross or upon any gutter on any street within the City of St. Maries, any wagon or other vehicle of any character whatsoever at a point or place other than the point or plece provided for such crossing, or cuase the same to be done.

## Section 5.

That any person, firm, or corporation, violat- ing any of the provisions of this ordinance shall be guilty of a misdemeanor, and upon conviction thereof shall be subject to a fine of not more than $100.00, or to imprisonment for thirty das, or both.

## Section 6.

All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

## Section 7.

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval, and publication.
