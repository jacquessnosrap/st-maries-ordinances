AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR THE CALLING AND HOLDING OF A SPECIAL ELECTION IN AND FOR THE CITY OF ST. ST. MARIES, BENEWAH COUNTY, IDAHO, ON Friesday THE 27th DAY OF July OF y A. D., 1915, 

FOR THE PURPOSE OF VOTING ON THE PROPOSITION OF ISSUING NEGOTIABLE MUNICIPAL COUPON BONDS OF SAID CITY TO AN 00 AMOUNT AGGREGATING THE PRINCIPAL SUM OF $ 37,000.

FOR THE PURPOSE OF SALE, AND TO APPLY THE PROCEEDS THEREOF TO THE ACQUIRING BY PURCHASE, OR OTHERWISE, A WATER WORKS PLANT FOR THE CITY OF ST. MARIES AND DAE WATER SUPPLY THEREFOR;

PROVIDING HOW AND IN WHAT MANNER SUCH BONDS SHALL BE ISSUED; AND

PROVIDING FOR AND DESIGNATING THE PROVISIONS TO BE MADE FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE PRINCIPAL AND INTEREST.

Whereas it is necessary in order to acquire a water supply for the City of St. Maries and its inhabitants, that said City of St. Maries acquire a water works plant for said eity,

And whereas there are no funds in the treasury of said City of St. Maries available for the purpose of acquiring by purchase, or otherwise, a water works plant for such municipality and the water supply thereför,

And whereas large benefits will accrue to the general public and to the citizens of said city by reason of the acquiring by said city, by purchase or otherwise,fa water works plant for such municipality and the water supply therefor,

And whereas it is deemed adviseable and hereby declared necess- ary that the funds required for said purpose shall be raised by the issuance and sale of municipal coupon bonds of said city to an amount aggregating the principal sum of $ 37,000,%

and

Whereas by virtue of Section 2315 of the Revised Codes of Idaho as amended by Chapter 147 of the 1913 Session Laws of the State of Idaho, and again as amended by Chapter 44 of the 1915 Pession, Laws of the State of Idaho, page 129, Sub-Division B theret, every city, town, or village incorporated under the laws of the territory of Idaho or of the State of Idaho, shall have power and authority to issue municipal coupon bonds in a sufficient amount to acquire, by purchase or otherwise, a water works plant for such municipality and a water supply therefor, the amount of which bonds shall be determined by the Council or Board of Trustees and stated in an ordinance therefor,

Now, Therefor, in consideration of the premises, BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, as follows:-

## Section 1

That a special election in and for the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, be and the same hereby is called to be held in the City of St. Maries, at the usual polling places in said city, to-wit, at F. B. Hay's building on for the First First Ward off said \# I building for the Second Ward of said City The Townsite Store building for the Third Ward of said City, on Tuesday to wit, the 27th day of July A. D. 1915, between the hours of eight o'clock A. M. and seven o'clock P. M., at which time there shall be sub- mitted to the qualified electors of the City of St. Maries, who are tax payers of said City, the proposition following, for their vote and determination thereat, to-wit:

"Shall the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah, County, Idaho, be authorized to issue municipal coupon bonds in the amount of $ 37,000. said bonds to bear interest semi-annually at a rate not exceeding six per centum per annum, and the principal of said bonds to be paid at the option of the City of St. Maries, at any time after ten (10) years and must be paid within twenty (20) years from the time they are issued; said bonds being for the purpose of ac- quiring, by purchase or otherwise, a water works plant for the City of St. Maries and a water supply therefor".
