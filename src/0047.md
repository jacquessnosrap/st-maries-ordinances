# Ordinance 47 

An ordinance fixing and establishing the official grade and elevation as that portion of College Avenue commencing at the center line of First Street and extending west to the west line of Sixth Street. 

BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CHAIRMAN AND BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE VILLAGE OF ST. MARIES, IDAHO:

## Section 1
That the official grade and elevation of that portion of College Avenue commencing at the center line of First Street and extending west to the west line of Sixth Street be and the same is hereby fixed and established as follows: at the center line of First Street .77 feet below city base; at the east line of the intersection of Second Street and College Avenue 16.55 feet above city base; at the west line of the intersection of Second Street and College Avenue 18.70 feet above city base; at the east line of the intersection of Third Street and College Avenue 27.20 feet above city base; at the west line of the intersection of Third Street and College Avenue 30.20 feet above city base; at the east line of the intersection of Fourth Street and College Avenue 38.20 feet above city base; at the west line of the intersection of Fourth Street and College Avenue 40.70 feet above city base; at the east line of the intersection of Fifth Street and College Avenue 48.70 feet above city base; at the west line of the intersection of Fifth Street and College Avenue 50.38 feet above city base; at the east line of the intersection of Sixth Street and College Avenue 54.70 feet above city base and at the west line of the intersection of Sixth Street and College Avenue 55.70 feet above city base.

## Section 2
That this ordinance shall take effect and be in force after its passage, approval and publication.

PASSED and APPROVED this 17th day of August, 1907.

Attest: Thomas C White
Village Clerk.


Fred Russel
Acting Chairman.
