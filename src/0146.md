AN ORDINANCE AMENDING SECTION 18, SECTION 20, AND SECTION 26, OF ORDINANCE NO.140 OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, KOOTENAI COUNTY, IDAHO, BEING ENTITLED "An ordinance creating the Local Sewerage Improvement District Number Five in the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, dofining the boundaries thereof and providing for the construction of a sewerage system therein; providing for the appointment of a Sewer Committee and defining and regulating their powers and duties; providing for the levying of a special assessment for the sewerage improvement and the expenses connected therewithand providing for the issuing of bonds therefor; providing for the payment of said assessment in ten (10) equal an nual installments and for the collection thereof; providing for the redemption of said bonds; providing for an assesment of the property in said Local Improvement District Number Five and for the preparation of an assesment roll and for the hearing of protests against the same and providing for the repeal of all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith".

Be It Ordained, by the Mayor and Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho,

## Section I.

That Section 18 of Ordinance No.140 of the City of St. Maries, being entitled " AN ORDINANCE CREATING THE LOCAL SEWERAGE IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NUMBER FIVE IN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, KOOTENAI COUNTY, IDAHO DEFINING THE BOUNDARIES THEREOF AND PROVIDING FOR THE CONSTRUCTION OF A SEWERAGE SYSTEM THEREIN PROVIDING FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF A SEWER COMITTEE AND DEFINING AND REGULATING THEIR POWERS AND DUTIES; PROVIDING FOR THE LEVYING OF A SPECIAL ASSESSMENT FOR THE SEWERAGE IMPROVEMENT AND THE EXPENSES CONNECTED THEREWITH AND PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUING OF BONDS THEREFOR; PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENTOF SAID ASSESSMENT IN TEN (10) EQUAL ANNUAL INSTALLMENTS AND FOR THE COLLECTION THEREOF; PROVIDING FOR THE REDEMTION OF SAID BONDS; PROVIDING FOR AN ASSESSMENT OF THE PROTERTY IN SAID LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NUMBER FIVE AND FOR THE PREPARATION OF AN ASSESSMENT ROLL AND THE HEARING OF PROTESTS AGAINST THE SAME AND PROVIDING FOR THE REPEAL OF ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH" be and hereby is, amended to read as follows:- "That said bonds shall be payable on or before the first day of November, A.D., 1923, and shall bear interest at the rate of eight (8) per cent per annum, which said interest shall be payable amually on the first day of November in each year at the office of the City Treasurer of said City, or at some banking house in New York City, New York, at the option of the purchaser thereof, Said bonds shallbe issued in denominations of five Hundred (500) Dollars each, and shall be numbered from one upward, consecutively, and each bond and coupon shall be signed by the mayor and t attested by the Clerk of said City, provided however, that said coupons may, in lieu of being so signed, have the fac- simile of the signature of such officer, and each bond shall have the seal of said city affixed thereto, and shall refer to the improvement for the paymentof which the same shall be issued."

## Section II.

That Section 20 of said Ordinance No. 140 of the City of St.Marics, be and hereby is amended to read as follows:- "The assessment roll hereinbefore provided for when approved, shall be the asessment of the costs and expenses of the improvement herein against the lots, pieces, and parcels of land included within said Local Sewerage Improvement District No.5 for which the bonds herein provided shall be issued. The assessment levied against each piece or parcel or parcel of land may be paid in ten equal annual installments with interest upon thw whole sun so charged as an assessment and unpaid, at the rate of eight (8) per cent per annum and the first installment she be due and collectable on the first day of September, A.D., 1914, and each installment thereafter shall be due on the first day of said month in each succeeding year thereafter, and shall become delinquent twenty days after becoming due. The amount which shall be payable and collect- able each year shall be one of such equal annual installments with the interest due thereon, together with the interests on all installments thereafter to become due, and the whole thereof shall be collected as a soparate tax and in the manner provided by law for the collection of assessments for such improvements where no bonds are issued.

## Section III.

That Section 26 of said Ordinance No. 140 of the City of St. Maries, be, and the same hereby is, amended to read as follows:- "The city treasurer shall cause to be published a notice in the official newspaper of the city during each issue thereof in the month of August in each year during which said bonds shall run, that an installment, together with the inter- est upon the unpaid installments, will be due upon the first day of September and delinquent twenty days thereafter, and unless paid within twenty days thereafter will be collected in the manner provided by law in case of failure to pay the same. And the said city treasurer shall mail to the owner or owners, if known, of cach piece or parcel of land, a notice that the installment together with the interest is due and payable on said date, but a failure of such owner or owners toreceive such notice or the failure of the said treasurer to mail or publish the same, shall not be taken or held to prevent the collection of the same as provided herein and by law

## Section IV.

That Sections Eighteen, Twenty, and Twenty-Six of said Ordinance Number 140, and all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed and rendered of no force or effect.

## Section V.

That this ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval, and publication in one issue of the St. Maries Record, a biewoekly newspaper of general circulation published in St.Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.
