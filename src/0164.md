AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING THAT THE CITY TREASURER SHALL GIVE A BOND TO THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, AND FIXING THE AMOUNT ON SUCH BOND;

PROVIDING PLACES OF DEPOSIT WHEREIN SAID CITY TREASURER SHALL DEPOSIT THE MONEYS OF SAID CITY AND 

PROVIDING THAT SAID CITY TREASURER SHALL TAKE BONDS OR SECURITIES FROM SAID PLACES OF DEPOSIT, AND 

FIXING THE AMOUNT OF SAID BONDS OR SECURITIES.

Be it ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, as follows:-

## Section 1.

That the City Treasurer of the City of St. Haries, be and he hereby is required, to furnish a bond, good and sufficient sureties thereon to be approved by the City Council of said City, running to the City of St. Maries, and conditioned upon the faithful performance of his trust as such City Treasurer and for the faithful accounting and safe keeping of the moneys belonging to said City. Said bond shall be in such penal sum as the City Council shall from time to time determine by resolution, which penal sum shall be not less than five Thousand ($5,000) Dollars at any one time, and shall be at all times sufficient to save the corporation from any loss. Said bond shall be furnished by the City Treasurer immediately after his election and before entering upon the duties of his office, and shall be one of the requisite conditions of his qualification.

## Section 2.

The City Treasurer of the City of St. Maries shall keep all moneys in his hands belonging to said municipality in any regularly organized bank within the corporate limite of the City of St. Maries, Idaho. Provided, however, that before depositing any moneys belonging to the corporation in any bank said City Treasurer shall require from said Bank securities, or a bond, with good and sufficient sureties thereon to be approved by the City Council, in the penal sum of not less than $5,000 at any one time, and at all times in a sum sufficient to save the corporation and the City Treasurer from any loss. whenever said City Treasurer shall deem it necessary for the protection of the City or himself, he may with the consent of the City Council, require from any bank in which corporate money is deposited, an additional bond or securities in an amount equal to the amount of moneys deposited with said Bank.

## Section 3.

All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

## Section 4.

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval and publication.

Passed the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, this 18th day of May, A. D., 1915.

Approved this 18th day of May, A. D., 1915.

Attest:
Jas. R. Schulte City Clerk.
E. Hailey Mayor.
