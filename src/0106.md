An ordinance amending section 18 and section 20 of ordinance, No. 98 of the village of St. Maries Kootenai county, Idaho, being entitled "An ordinance creating local sewerage improvement district No. 2 in the village of St. Maries, Kootenai county, Idaho, defining the boundaries thereof and providing for the construction of a sewerage system therein; 

providing for the appointinent of a sewer committee and defining and regulat- ing their powers and duties;

providing for the levying of special assessment for the sewerage improvement, the expense connected therewith and providing for the issning of bonds therefor: providing for ding for the payment of said assessment in ten equal annual installments and for the collec- tion thereof;

prescribing the duties of the village clerk and village treasurer in connection with the collection thereof: providing for the redemp- tion of said bonds;

providing for an assessment of the property in said local sewerage improvement district No. 2, and for the preparation of an assessment roll and for the hearing of protests against the same and providing for the repealing of all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith," and amending and repealing said sections 18 and 20 of said ordinance No. 98 of the village of St. Maries, and all other ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith. 

This ordinance shall be in force and effect from and after its passage and publication. 

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries. Kootenai County, Idaho.

## Section 1

That section No. 18 of ordinance No. 98 of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai county, Idaho, being entitled "An ordinance cre- ating Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 2, in the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai county Idaho, defining the boundaries thereof and pro- viding for the construction of a sewerage system therein; providing for the appointment of a sew- er committee and defining and regulating their powers and duties; providing for the levying of a special assessment for the sewerage improve- ment and the expenses connected therewith and providing for the issuing of bonds therefor: pro- viding for the payment of said assessment in ten equal annual installinents and for the collection thereof; prescribing the duties of the village clerk and the village treasurer in connection with the collection thereof: providing for the redemption of said bonds; providing for the as, sessment of property in said Local Sewerage im- provement district No. 2. and for the preparation of an assessment roll and for the hearing of pro- tests against the same and providing for the re- pealing of all ordinances and parts of ordinaces in conflict herewith," be amended to read as fol- lows: "The said bonds shall be payable on or before the 1st day of October A. D. 1921, and shall bear interest at the rate of eight per cent per annum, which said interest shall be payable annually on the first day of October in each year at the office of the village treasurer or some banking house in the city of New York, at the option of the purchaser thereof. Said bonds shall be issued in denominations of $500.00 each, and shall be num- bered from one upward consecutively, and each bond and coupon shall be signed by the chair- man of the board of trustees and attested by the clerk of the village; providing, however, that said coupon may, in lieu of being so signed have printed thereon the facsimile of the signatures of such officers, and each bond shall have the seal of the village affixed thereto and shall refer to the improvement for the payment of which the same shall be issued."

## Section 2

That section 20 of said orninance No. 98 of the village of St. Maries, Kootenai coun- ty, Idaho, be amended to read as follows: "The assessment roll hereinbefore inentioned, when approved, shall be the assessment of the costs and expenses of improvement herein a- gainst the lots, pieces and parcels of land includ- ed within said Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 2. for which the bonds herein pro- vided shall be issued. The assessment levied a- gainst each piece or parcel of land shall be paid in ten (10) equal annual installments with inter- est upon the whole sum so charged as an assess- ment and unpaid at the rate of eight per cent per annun, and the first installment shall be due and collectible on the first day of August, A. D. 1912, and each annual installment there- after shall be due on the first day of said month in each succeeding year thereafter and shall be- come delinquent twenty days after becoming due The amount which shall be payable and collect- ible each year shall be one of such equal annual installments with the interest due thereon, to- gether with the interest on all installments there- after to become due and the whole thereof shall be collected as a separate tax and in the manner pro provided by law for the collection of asses ments for such improvements where no bonds are is- sued."

## Section 3

18 and 20 of said ordinance No. 28 of the village of St. Maries, and all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are here- by repealed and rendered of no further force and effect.

## Section 4

This ordinance shall become and be in full force and effect from and after its passage, approval and puplication in one issue of the St. Maries Gazette, a weekly newspaper of general tirculation published in the Village of St. Maries. Kootenai County. Idaho.

Approved this 10th day of September, A. D. 1911.

O. E. HAILEY Chairman Board Trustees.
