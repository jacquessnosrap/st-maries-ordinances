An Ordinance disposing of all of River street, K street, L street, M street, and the alleys in blocks two (2), four (4) and seven (7), in the Townsite of St. Maries, Idaho, according to the official plat thereof on file in the General Land office at Washington, D. C., and in the office of the Recorder of Kootenai Co., Idaho, also all those certain portions of West Boundary Street, First Street and Second Street, which lie north of the produced north line of the alleys in block five (5), six (6) and seven (7) in the aforesaid townsite of St. Maries, Idaho, and also the West forty feet (40) of that portion of Third street, which lies north of the produced north line of I street, in the aforesaid townsite of St. Maries, Idaho, and directing a conveyance thereof to be executed by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, attested by the Clerk thereof, wherein the Milwaukee Land Company, a corporation authorized to do business in the State of Idaho, shall be the grantee, and to whom said conveyance shall be delivered.

Whereas the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, did by ordinance No. 124, duly passed on August 19, A. D. 1912, and published on August 23, 1912, in one issue of the St. Maries Gazette, a newspaper of general circulation published in the said village, vacate the streets, alleys, and portions of streets, described in the title hereof.

Now Therefore be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho.

## Section 1

That the chairman of the Board of Trustees be auth- orized and directed to execute and deliver, and the Clerk thereof be directed to attest a deed of conveyance, granting and conveying to the Milwaukee Land Company a corporation authorized to do business in the State of Idaho, the following described properties situated in Kootenai County, Idaho, to-wit:

All of River Street, K. Street, L. Street, M. Street, and the Alleys in Blocks Two (2) Four (4) and Seven (7) in the townsite of St Maries, Idaho, according to the official plat thereof on file in the General Land Office at Washington, D.C., and in the office of the Recorder of Kootenai County, Idaho, also all those certain portions of West Boundary Street, First Street and Second Street, which lie north of the produced north line of the Alleys in blocks five (5) six (6) and seven (7) in the aforesaid townsite of St. Maries, Idaho; and also the West forty feet (40) of that portion of Third Street which lies north of the produced north line of I Street, in the afore- said townsite of St. Maries, Idaho. Provided however, that said vil- lage, shall have the right to lay and maintain sewer and water pipes in any of the aforesaid vacated streets and alleys, which shall not interfere with the lands upon which said mill-building is located, and shall also have full right and power to enter said premises to lay repair or maintain the same.

## Section 2

This ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and afterthe publication thereof.

Passed the Board of Trustees this 7th day of April, 1913.

CHARIES BREBNER
Chairman of Board of Trustees
Attest: Chas. R. Schulte, Clerk
