stating the general character of said proposed street improvements;

stating the estimated cost and expense of said proposed street improvements and stating that the cost and expense of said proposed street improvements are to be assessed against all the property abbutting, fronting, contiguous or tributary to (and included in the assessment district to be provided) the said portions of said described streets and avenues, except that portion of the cost and expense required to make said improvements in the spaces formed by the junction of two or more streets, or wherein one main street terminates in or crosses another main street, and required to put in and replace the necessary cross walks, and required to lower the water main and pipes where necessary to permit of said grading, and required to raise or lower the sewer man-holes where necessary to permit of said grading, which cost and expense is to be paid by the city at large, and fixing the time within which protests may be filed in the office of the City Clerk;

repealing all ordinances or parte of ordinances in conflict with this ordinance.

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Kootensi County, State of Idaho.

## Section 1.

That it is the intention of the City Council of the city of St. Maries, in the County of Kootenai, State of Idaho, to improve the street known as NINTH STREET, in said city. . . That the estimated cost and expense of putting inhand replacing the necessary cross-walks is $30.00. That the estimated cost and expense of lowering the sewer man-holes where necessary to permit of said grading is $4.00. That the estimated cost and expense of Engineering, and Attorney's fees and incidentals is $900.00. That of the above total estimated cost and expense of all the improvements on the above described portions of said streets and avenues, the estimated cost and expense to be paid by the city of St. Maries is $587.92.

## Section 4.

That the cost and expense of the proposed improvements on the above described portions of said streest, and avenues is to be assessed against the property abutting, contiguous or tributary to (and included in the improvement district to be provided) the above described portions of said above named streets and avenues proposed to be improved, except that the cost and expense of all improvements made in the spaces formed by the junction of two or more streets or where one main street terminates in or crosses another main street and of all crossings and cross walks at the corners and intersections of streets and the expense incurred in lowering water mains and pipes where necessary to permit of said grading and the expense incurred in lowering or raising sewer man-holes where necessary to permit of said grading shall be paid by the city of St. Maries.

## Section 5.

That any person desiring to protest against the said proposed improvements shall file their written protests thereto in the office of Chas. R. Schulte, the City Clerk of the City of St. Maries, on or before six o'clock P. M. on the 25th day of November, 1913.

## Section 6.

That all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict with this ordinance are hereby repealed.

## Section 7.

That this ordinance shall take effect and be in full force upon its passage, approval and publication in two consecutive issues of the "Record", a bi-weekly newspaper of general circultaion published in the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, on Tuesday and Friday of each and every week, the official newspaper of the City of St. Maries.

Henry J. Mulcahy, Mayor.
