An ordinance repealing Section Ten (10) of Ordinance No. Six (6) of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, being entitled, "An ordinance prohibiting bawdy houses and disorderly conduct, defining what shall be considered such, and providing for penalies for violation thereof.

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor andCouncil of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:-

## Section 1

That Section ten (10) of Ordinance Nor Six (6) of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, be and the same is hereby repealed.

## Section 2

That this ordinance shall be in full force and effect from and after its passage, approval and public ation in one issue of the "Record" a newspaper of general circulation in the City of St. Maries, Idaho.

Passed its first reading at an adjourned regular meet- ing of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on November 4th, 1913.

Passed its second reading, under suspension of the rules, at an adjourned regular meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on November 4th, 1913.

Passed its third and final reading, under suspension of the rules, at an adjourned regular meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, and duly enacted an ordinance of said city on November 4th, 1973.

Approved and passed over the Mayor's veto, by a vote of two-thirds of the members of the Council elected, this 1st day of December, 1913.

Attest:-

Chas. R. Schulte, City Clerk.

Publiches in "The Record" Dec. 16, 1913.
