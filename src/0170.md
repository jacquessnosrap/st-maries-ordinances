STATING THE METHOD BY WHICH THE EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS IN THE SPACE FORMED BY THE JUNCTION OF TWO OR MORE STREETS, OR WHEREIN ONE MAIN STREET TERMINATES IN OR CROSSES ANOTHER MAIN STREET, AND ALSO OF ALL STREET CROSSINGS OR CROSS WALKS, AND ALSO IN THE SPACE OPPOSITE ALLEYS, IS TO BE PAID, WHICH COST AND EXPENSE IS TO BE ASSESSED AGAINST ALL THE PROPERTY IN SUCH IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT IN PROPORTION TO THE BENEFITS DERIVED, AND STATING THAT THE EXPENSE REQUIRED TO LOWER THE WATER MAINS AND PIPES WHERE NECESSARY TO PERMIT OF SAID GRADING AND REQUIRED TO RAISE OR LOWER THE SEWER MANHOLES WHERE NECESSARY TO PERMIT OF SAID GRADING, IS TO BE PAID BY THE CITY; 

AND FIXING THE TIME IN WHICH PROTESTS MAY BE FILED IN THE OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK OF SAID CITY, AND REPEALING ALL ORIDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT WITH THIS ORDINANCE.

BE IT ORDAINED, by the mayor and city council of the city of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:

## Section 1.

That it is the intention of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, to improve that portion of Fourteenth Street in the City of St. Maries from the South boundary line of the City of St. Maries to the south line of College Avenue, and that portion of Thirteenth Streets from the south boundary line of the City of St. Maries to the south line of College Avenue, and that portion of Twelfth Street from the south boundary line of the City of St. Maries to the South line of College Avenue, and that portion of Eleventh Street from the south boundary line of the City of St. Maries to the south line of College Avenue, and that portion of Tenth Street from the South boundary line of the City of Pt. Maries to the south line of College Avenue, and that portion of Ninth Street from the south boundary line of the City of St. Maries to the south line of College Avenue, and that portion of College Avenue from the west line of Tenth Street to the West line of Fourteenth Street, and that portion of Jefferson Avenue from the West line of Ninth Street to the East line of Fourteenth Street, and that portion of Washington Avenue from the West line of Ninth Street to the East line of Fourteenth Street, by grading said portions of said streets and avenues and curbing them with concrete curbing and putting in the necessary cross walks, lowering the water pipes where necessary to permit of said grading and raising or lowering the sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading, putting in retaining walls where necessary to permit of said grading, and by moving such telephone and electric light poles as it may be necessary to move to permit of said grading, and by making whatever other incidental improvements that may be necessary to permit of said grading.

## Section 2:

That the general character of said proposed improvements on the above described portions of said streets and avenues shall consist of grading them, and curbing them with concrete curbing and putting in the necessary cross walks, lowering the water pipes where necessary to permit of said grad- ing and raising or lowering the sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading, moving such telephone and electric light poles as it may be necessary to move to permit of said grading and making such other incidental improvements as may be necessary to permit of said grading. Said grading to be done in accordance with the grade heretofore established by ordinance of said city and in accordance with the profiles now on file with the City Engineer of the City of St. Maries.

## Section 4.

That the cost and expense of the said proposed improvements on the above described portions of said streets and avenues is to be assessed against the property abutting, fronting, contiguous or tributary (and included in the improvement district to be provided) to the above described portions of the above named streets and avenues proposed to be improved, except that the cost and expense of lowering the water mains and pipes where necessary to permit of said grading and the cost and expense required to raise or lower and sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading, shall be paid by the city of St. Maries at large. The expense of the improvements in the space formed by the junc- tion of two or more streets and the expense of the improvements in the space formed by the intersection of streets, and the expense of the improvements in the space wherein one main street terminates in or crosses another main street and the expense of the improve- ments in the space opposite alleys, and the expense of all street crossings or cross walks is to be assessed against all the property in such improvement district to be provided, in proportion to the benefits derived.

## Section 5.

That any person or persons desiring to protest against the said proposed improvements shall file a written pro- test thereto in the office of Charles R. Schulte, City Clerk of the City of St. Maries on or before five o'clock P. M. on the 10th day of August A. D., 1915.

## Section 6.

That all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

## Section 7.

That this ordinance shall take effect and be in full force upon its passage, approval and publication in two consecutive issues of the St. Maries Gazefte a weekly newspaper of general circulation published in the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, the official newspaper of said city.
