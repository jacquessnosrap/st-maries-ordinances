# Ordinance No. 131

An Ordinance Declaring That The City Clerk of the City of St. Maries Shall Exofficio Be Police Judge Thereof. 

Whereas, the Village of St. Maries has in a manner provided by law been duly and properly organized into a city of the second class under the laws of the State of Idaho, and 

whereas after the next ensuing general municipal election to be held in said municipality said village will have all the powers and duties of a city of the second class, and 

Whereas the officers to be elected at said municipal election will be such officers as are provided for in cities of the second class, 

Now Therefore, by virtue of Section. 2186 of the Revised Codes of the State of Idaho, Be It Ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That the city clerk of the city of St. Maries shall be ex-officio Police Judge thereof.

## Section 2

This ordinance hall take effect at such time, after its passage, ap proval, and publication, and before the next ensuing general municipal election of the said municipality, as may be necessary to place the provisions hereof into effect. 

Passed by the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, this 8th day of March, A. D. 1913. 

Approved by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, this 8th day of March, A. D. 1913.

CHARLES BRERNER Chairman Pro. tem 

Attest: CHAS. R. SCHULTE Village Clerk
