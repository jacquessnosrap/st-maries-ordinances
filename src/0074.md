# Ordinance 74 

An ordinance prescribing the place of meeting of the Board of trustees of the Village of St. Maries. 

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries:

## Section 1

The Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, until further order, shall convene regularly in the Town Hall in said Village, on the First Monday of each Month at 7.30 O'clock P.M. Special meetings may be called by the Chairman or by any three trustees, by notice in writing from the clerk served upon the other members of the Board.

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage approval and publication.

Approved this 16 day of August 1909.

Attest.
J. S. Turnbull
Village Clerk.

Perley Martin 
Chairman
