# Ordinance 19

An ordinance fixing the salaries of officers of the village of St Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and board of village trustees of the village of St Maries, Kootenai County, State of Idaho.

## Section 1
That the salaries of the officers of the village of St Maries shall be as follows:
Clerk $40.00 per year, Marshal $15.00 per month, Treasurer $25.00 per year.

## Section 2
This ordinance shall be in force and take effect from the first day of May 1903.

Approved May 4, 1903

JW Skelton
Chairman

Attest Thao C. White
Village Clerk
