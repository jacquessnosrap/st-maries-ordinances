# Ordinance 58

An Ordinance to amend an ordinance entitled "An Ordinance providing for the construction of sidewalks within the Village of St. Maries, and the penalty for failure to construct the same when ordered by the Board of Trustees of said Village" known as Ordinance No. 12 of Ordinances of the Village of St. Maries.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho;

## Section 1

That section 1 of Ordinance No. 12 passed and approved on the 6th day of November, 1902, be and the same is hereby amended to read as follows:

## Section 2

That all sidewalks hereafter constructed within it incorporated limits of the Village of St. Maries, shall be eight inches high laid above grade of the street, except by special permission of the board, and no sidewalk to be less than six feet wide. All sidewalks shall be constructed of planks two inches thick and eight inches wide, laid one-half inch apart, on not less than three stringers, which stringers shall be four inches thick and six inches wide.

Any sidewalk that is three feet or more from the level of the street must be protected by a hand-rail.

## Section 3

This Ordinance shall take effect and be in force after its passage, approval and publication.

Passed and approved this 8 day of August, 1908.

Attest
J. S. Turnbell
Clerk of the Board.

Perley Martin
Chairman of the the Board.
