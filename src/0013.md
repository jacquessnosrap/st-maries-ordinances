# Ordinance 13

An Ordinance providing for payment of fines and costs attaches therein in case of failure to pay in cash, by work on the Village streets.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Boardof Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, State of Idaho.

## Section 1
Every able bodied male person over the age of twelve years who shall be convicted of any violation of any ordinance of the Village of St. Maries, and who shall be committed to jail in default of the payment of said fine and costs may be required by the police magistrate to pay the amount of said fine and costs by labor on the streets of said Village at the rate of two dollars per day until such fine and costs shall be paid.

Approved Nov 6, 1902.

Attest. George Hawk Clerk,

J W Skelton
Chairman.
