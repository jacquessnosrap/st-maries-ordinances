AN ORDINANCE COMFIRMING AND APPROVING THE CREATION AND ESTABLISHMENT OF LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NO. 2 IN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, IDAHO;

COMFIRMING AND APPROVING THE ASSESSMENT ROLL FOR SAID DISTRICT AS FILED IN THE OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, WHICH ASSESSMENT ROLL WAS DULY AND REGULARLY CERTIFIED IN ACCORDANCE WITH LAW APPROVING AND COMFIRMING ALL PROCEEDINGS HAD IN CONNECTION WITH SAID ASSESSMENT ROLL;

PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENT BY INSTALLMENTS OF THE COST AND EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS MADE IN SAID DISTRICT;

PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF IMPROVEMENT BONDS OF THE DISTRICT, ASSESSING A PORTION OF THE COST OF SAID IMPROVEMENTS AGAINST THE LOTS, BLOCKS AND PARCELS OF LAND IN SAID DISTRICT CONTIGUOUS TO, FRONTING OR ABUTTING ON SAID IMPROVEMENTS, AND DESCRIBING THE MANNER OF COLLECTION AND PAYMENT OF SAID ASSESSMENTS.

Whereas the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, has heretofore pursuant to the requisite preliminary proceedings, and Ordinance No. 152 of said City, establishing Local Improvement District No. 2 of said City, for the purpose of improving certain streets and avenues in said City by grading, gravelling and curbing them with concrete gutter and making certain other incidental improvements as specified in said ordinance, and

Whereas the Committee on Streets and Alleys together with the City Engineer, have heretofore in strict conformity with law and the ordinances of said City, made out an assessment roll in and for said Local Improvement District No. 2, fixing the total cost of the improvements therein taxable against private property at $ 42,480.62 assessing said amount against the lots, blocks and parcels of land contiguous to, fronting or abutting upon said improvements in said district, said assessment being distributed in strict accordance with law (said amount being the total cost of said improvements, after deducting that part which is to be paid by the City of St. Maries out of its improvement or other funds), which assessment roll was filed with the City Clerk on the 18th day of January, 1915, and

Whereas said assessment roll was properly certified by the City Council in strict accordance with law, and

Whereas after receiving said assessment roll the City Clerk under the direction of the City Council gave notice by three successive publications in the official newspaper of the City of St. Maries that such assessment roll was on file in his office, giving the date of filing the same, and stating the time at which the City Council would hear and consider objections to said assess- ment roll by the parties aggrieved by said assessment, to wit, on the 10th day of February, 1915, and stating that the owner or owners of any property which was assessed in such assessment roll, whethe: named or not in such roll, might within ten days after the last publication of said notice file with the City Clerk his or her ob- jections in writing to the regularity of the proceedings in making such assessment and the correctness of such assessment or of the amount levied on any property, lot or parcel of land, and

Whereas the objections filed with the City Clerk, in accordance with law and the ordinances of said City and with the aforesaid notice, were the objections of one A. W. Wayne and Mrs. D. W. Olinger which objections were against the assessment levied upon property owned by said objectors and particularly described in said assess- ment roll, and

Whereas after considering all objections filed with the City Clerk, and after considering said assessment roll, the Council ratified and approved the same notwithstanding said ob- jections or any objections, and

Whereas it is the opinion of the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries that the cost and expense of the improve- ments made in said Local Improvement District No. 2 which are chargeable to the abutting, adjoining, contiguous or approximate property should be provided for and paid for by installments as specified in the ordinance creating said District No. 2, and

Whereas the Mayor and City Council of the said City desire to issue in the name of the City improvement bonds of the said district to bear date the day of March, AD, 1915, payable on or before Furst ten years,

Now, Therefore, be it Ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That all the proceedings, matters and things heretofore had, taken and done in connection with the establish- ment of Local Improvement District No. 2 in the City of St. Maries, Idaho, and the making and construction therein of the improvements consisting of the improving of certain streets and avenues and parts of streets and avenues in said Improvement District described, by grading them, gravelling them, curbing them with concrete curbing, putting in concrete gutters and the other incidental improvements particularly specified in Ordinance 152, and the apportionment of the cost of said improvements, except that part chargeable against the City, against the lots, blocks and parcels of land in said district, be and the same are hereby in all respects approved, ratified and confirmed.

## Section 2

That the assessments shown upon the assessment roll of Local Improvement District No. 2, for the improvement of the streets and parts of streets included therein as herein desig- nated and pursuant to Ordinance No. 152, be and the same are in all things approved and confirmed.

## Section 3

That each of the lots, tracts and parcels of land and other property shown and described upon the said assess- ment roll, is hereby declared to be specially benefitted by said improvement in at least the amount charged against the same, and that the assessments appearing against each lot, tract or parcel of land and other property is in proportion to the several assess- ments bearing upon said work. There is hereby levied and assessed against each lot, tract and parcel of land and other described property in said roll, the amount finally charged against the same thereon. Said assessment shall be payable in ten equal annual installments, the first of which shall become due and payable on the 15th day of January 1916, and each succeeding installment shall become due and payable on the same day in each year thereafter. Provided, however, that all or any portion of said installment may be paid without interest penalty costs within the time provided by law prior to the issuance of said bonds. All assessments should bear interest at the rate of seven per cent per annum. Provided, however, that if any property owner desires to redeem his property from liability by paying the entire assessment chargeable against the same after the issuance of the bonds, he may do so by paying all the installments of the assessments which have been levied and also the amount of unlevied installments with interest upon the latter at the rate of seven per cent per annum from the date of issuance of the bonds to the me of maturity of the las installment.

## Section 4

That there be issued in the name of the City of St. Maries, improvement district bonds of said Local Improvement District No. 2, which bonds shall bear date on the First day of March, A.D., 1915, and shall bear Interest at the rate of seven per cent per annum payable annually on the xirst day of March in each year. • Said inter- est shall be evidenced by coupons attached to said bonds; said bonds shall be numbered in consecutive numbers commencing with one and shall be in denominations of $ Two Hundred Dollars each except the first bond, which may be in such suitable amount as may be required to make the total issue conform with the amount hereby authorized. Said bonds and the coupons thereto attached shall be payable at the office of the City Treasurer of the City of St. Maries. Said bonds shall be signed by the Mayor and attested by the City Clerk, and counter-signed by the City Treasurer of said City, and shall be sealed with the corporate seal of said City; and all interest coupons, made in lieu of the original signature, bear the engraved fac simile signature of the Mayor, City Clerk and City Treasurer of said City, and when so executed will consti- tute binding obligations on said district for said interest.

## Section 5

That the said bonds when executed shall be issued to the contractors constructing the improvements in said Local Improvement District No. 2, in payment thereof, and when so issued shall transfer to the contractors for any owner or holder thereof all the right and interest of the City of St. Maries, in and with respect to each assessment against the property in said district liable to assessment for such local improvement, and the lien thereby created against the property in said district liable to assessment for such local improvements, and shall authorize said contractor and his assigns and the owner and holders of said bonds to receive, sue for and collect or have collected such assessments embraced in such bonds, or through any of the methods provided by law for the collection of assessments for local improvements, and the City of St. Maries hereby pledges to such bond holder, the exercise of all lawful corporate powers in the collection on of the assessments levied for the payment of such bonds.

## Section 6

Said assessment together with the interest thereon are hereby declared to be a lien in the several amounts assessed against each lot, tract or parcel of land upon said assessment roll from and after the publication of this ordinance, and shall have priority over all other liens except general taxes.

## Section 7

The City Council or other lawfully authorized taxing officers of St. Maries, shall, for the purpose of ratifying and confirming the assessments hereby made, levy special assess- ments each year hereafter sufficient to redeem the installments of bonds with interest, next thereafter maturity, as issued pur- suant to the provision of this ordinance, and in computing the amount of special assessments to be thus levied against each piece of property liable therefor, interest at the rate of seven per cent per annum from the date of the issuance of said bonds until the maturity of the installments of said bonds next there- after maturing shall be included in such levy.

## Section 8

The Mayor, City Clerk and City Treasurer are hereby authorized and directed to execute and deliver said bonds to the Contractor executing the improvement 1915, excepting those which may be can- celled by the City Treasurer pursuant to payment made upon his redemption notice given in accordance with law, and the fund of redemptions made, delivered to the contractor.

## Section 9

That all ordinances or parts of ordinance in conflict with this ordinance, are, in so far as they conflict with the provisions of this ordinance, hereby repealed.

Passed its first reading at a special meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on the 16th day of February A. D., 1915.

Passed its second reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, at an Special adjourned meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on the 17th day of February, A. D., 1915.

Passed its third and final reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, and duly enacted an ordinance of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, at a meeting of the City Council of St. Maries held on the 17th day of February A. D., 1915.

Approved this 17th day of February A. D., 1915.
