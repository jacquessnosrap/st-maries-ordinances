AN ORDINANCE AMENDING SECTIONS 5, 9, and 39 OF ORDINANCE NO. 183 OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES BY PROVIDING THAT ALL PAYMENTS FOR WATER CHARGES, FINES AND PENALTIES SHALL BE PAID TO THE CITY TREASURER.

Be It Ordained by the Mayor and the City Council of the City of St. Maries:-

## Section 1

That Section 5 of Ordinance No. 183 of the City of St. Maries be amanded to read as follows to-wit:-

Section 5. The City Clerk of the City of St. Maries, shall be the clerk of the Water Department of the City of St. Maries, and he shall do all the clerical work necessary for the conducting of such department, including the sending out of water bills and not- ices to water consumers. It shall be the duty of the City Clerk on the first day of every month, or as soon thereafter as may be practicable, to deliver to the City Treasurer a statement contain- ing the names of all water users and consumers, the amount of curr- ent water rent charged to each of such consumers, together with the amount of delinquent payments charged against each of such con- sumers.

All water charges, fines and penalties shall be paid to the City Treasurer of the City of St. Maries, provided, however, that the Water Commissioner may recieve payment of fines or delinquent water charges, but he shall upon receipt of the same forthwith pay the same over to the City Treasurer who shall make the proper notations upon the records.

The Water Commissioner shall at all times be the head of the Water Department of the City of St. Maries and subject only to the orders of the City Council and the water committee thereof, and the water commissioner, together with his assistant shall perform the duties herein de scribed for the water department of the City of St.maries except such duties as may be specifically assign- ed to the City Clerk or City Treasurer.

## Section 2

That Section 9 of Ordinance 183 of the City of St.Maries be amended to read as follows:

Section 9. All bills for water whether by flat rate or meter are due and payable at the office of the City Treasurer on the First day of each month for all water used the preceding month, and will become delinquent on the 10th day of the month, and if still delinquent on the 15th day of the month, the water will be shut off, and shall not be turned on until all bills are paid, including an additional charge of One$1.00) Dollar for turning on. Owners of property and the property served will be held liable for all water and service charges. Payments will be accepted from tenants, but that will not relieve the owner of the property if tenant becomes delinquent.

## Section 3

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval and publication and from and after the first day of May, 1917.

Passed by the City Council of the City of St. Maries, this thday of March A.D. 1917.

Approved this 5th day of March A.D.1917.

Attest: Lao P. Schulti
City Clerk.

Hailey
Mayor.
