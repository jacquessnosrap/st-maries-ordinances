An ordinance declaring the intention of the board of trustees of the village of St. Maries, Idaho, to improve a portion of said village by constructing therein a sewer system, defining the specific boundaries in which said proposed sewer system is to be constructed, stating the general character of the said sewer system, the estimated cost of the same and providing that the cost of the same is to be assessed on all property within such sewerage district, and providing further, that such sewerage district shall not include for assessinent, property occupied by streets, cross streets, and alleys within said district, and fixing the time in which protests against said improvements may be filed in the office of the village clerk, of said village.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the village of St. Maries, Kootenai county. Idaho.

## Section 2
The said district shall be kuown as Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 2, and to include therein all the property or properties within the boundaries stated in section one of this ordinance.

## Section 3
The general character of the said proposed sewer system is as follows. to-wit: A gravity system, the laterals and mains of which are to be placed in the streets and avenues within said district. The pipe of said sewer system to be vitrified sewer pipe and the said mains and laterals to be laid as follows, to wit: 

The main or trunk sewer is to be laid along College avenue from Ninth street to First street, thence north along First street to St Joe river; and shall consistof 12 inch pipe;

laterals are to be laid from the south boundary of said district along First, Second, Third, Sixth, Seventh, Eighth and Ninth streets to the said main or trunk sewer, and shall consist of 6 inch pipe;

the laterals along Fourth and Fifth streets shall extend from Washington avenue to said main or trunk sewer. and shall consist of 6 inch pipe;

a lateral is to be laid along Washington avenue from Eleventh street to the lateral on Ninth street and shall consist of 6 inch pipe;

a lateral is to be laid along Tenth street from the south boundary of said district to the lateral on Washington avenue, and shall consist of 6 inch pipe. The location and sizes of pipe herein, are herein given for the purpose of stating the general character of the systein proposed and are subject to such change and alteration as may be necessary in the proper construction and installation of such system.

## Section 4
The estimated cost of said proposed system is the sum of $20,000.

## Section 5
The costs of said proposed sewer system is to be assessed against all property included within said Local Sewerage Improvement District, provided, however, that the streets, alleys, and cross streets therein are not included for assessment.

## Section 6
Protests against the said proposed sewer system shall be filed in the office of Chas. R. Schulte, Village Clerk of said village, on or before 6 o'clock pm. on the 31st day of March A. D., 1911.

## Section 7
This ordinance shall be in force from and after its passage, approval and publication as s provided by law. Passed by the Board of Trustees the 6th day of March, A. D., 1911. Approved by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees the 6th day of March, A. D., 1911.
