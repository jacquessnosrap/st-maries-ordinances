AN ORDINANCE CREATING AND ESTABLISHING "LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NO. 4", CITY OF ST. MARIES, BENEWAH COUNTY, IDAHO, DESIGNATING THE PROPERTY INCLUDED THEREIN, NAMING THE STREETS AND AVENUES THEREIN AND DESIGNATING THE POINTS BETWEEN WHICH SAID IMPROVEMENTS ARE TO BE MADE;

PROVIDING FOR THE IMPROVEMENT OF SAID PORTIONS OF SAID STREETS AND AVENUES BY GRADING THEM AND DRAINING THE SAME BY A DRAINAGE DITCH, AND MAKING SUCH OTHER INCIDENTAL IMPROVEMENTS AS MAY BE NECESSARY TO PERMIT OF SAID GRADING;

PROVIDING HOW AND THE MANNER IN WHICH SAID IMPROVEMENTS SHALL BE MADE, ORDERING THE MAKING OF SAID IMPROVEMENTS AND PROVIDING THAT THE COST AND EXPENSE THEREOF, EXCLUDING THAT PORTION TO BE PAID BY THE CITY AT LARGE, BUT INCLUDING THE EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS IN THE SPACE FORMED BY THE JUNCTION OF TWO OR MORE STREETS AND THE EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS IN THE SPACE FORMED BY THE INTERSECTION OF THE STREETS AND THE EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS IN THE SPACE WHEREIN ONE MAIN STREET TERMINATES IN OR CROSSES ANOTHER MAIN STREET AND THE EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS IN THE SPACE OPPOSITE ALLEYS AND THE EXPENSE OF ALL THE STREET CROSSINGS OR CROSSWALKS, SHALL BE TAXED AND ASSESSED UPON ALL THE PROPERTY IN SAID IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT;

PROVIDING THE MANNER OF THE ASSESSMENT OF SUCH COST AND EXPENSE UPON THE PROPERTY MADE LIABLE THEREFOR;

PROVIDING THAT A PORTION OF THE COST OF SAID IMPROVEMENTS SHALL BE PAID BY THE CITY OF ST. MARIES AND PROVIDING WHAT PORTION OF THE COST OF SAID IMPROVEMENTS SHALL BE PAID BY THE PROPERTY WITHIN SAID DISTRICT;

PROVIDING THAT THE COST AND EXPENSE OF SAID WORK SO ASSESSED ON THE LOTS AND LANDS IN SAID LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT SHALL BECOME A LIEN UPON THE SAME AND PROVIDING FOR THE MANNER OF THE FORECLOSURE OF SUCH LIENS PROVIDING FOR THE PREPARING OF AN ASSESSMENT ROLL AND DEFINING THE DUTIES AND POWERS OF THE STREET COMMITTEE AND CITY ENGINEER IN CONNECTION THEREWITH;

PROVIDING FOR THE MANNER IN WHICH SAID ASSESSMENT ROLL SHALL BE MADE, ITS CORRECTION, REVISION, CONFIRMATION AND FOR THE MAKING OF A NEW ASSESSMENT ROLL IN CASE THE SAME BECOMES NECESSARY;

PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE COST AND EXPENSE OF SAID IMPROVEMENTS WHICH ARE CHARGEABLE TO THE ABUTTING, CONTIGUOUS, ADJOINING, OR APPROXIMATE PROPERTY IN TEN EQUAL ANNUAL INSTALIMENTS AND AUTHORIZING AND PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE IN THE NAME OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES OF SPECIAL ASSESS- MENT IMPROVEMENT BONDS OF SAID LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT AND PRO- VIDING FOR THE COLLECTION OF SAID SPECIAL TAX ASSESSMENT;

AND PROVIDING FOR THE REDEMPTION OF SAID BONDS; AND PROVIDING FOR THE REPEAL OF ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT WITH THIS ORDINANCE.

WHEREAS there has been presented to the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, a petition praying for the formation and establishment of a local improvement district to include the streets and avenues hereinafter described and the property con- tiguous, abutting and tributary thereto, and praying that in addition to the other costs and expenses of the work done in such improvement district the cost and expense of all improvements in the spaces formed by the junction of two or more streets, or wherein one main street terminates in or crosses another main street, and also the cost and expense of all street crossings or crosswalks be assessed against all the property in the improvement district in proportion to the benefits derived,

AND WHEREAS said petition was and is signed by seventy (70) per cent of the resident owners of property within such improvement district subject to assessment for the work in such district, NOWO THEREFORE,

Be it ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:-

## Section 1

That there is hereby established, "Local Improve- ment District No. 4" of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, which district includes all the property fronting or abutting on, contiguous or tributary to the streets to be improved between the points named and designated in Ordinance 171, which Ordinance No. 171 was duly passed by the City Council of the City of St. Maries on the 9th day of July, A. D., 1915, and approved by the Mayor of said City on said date and thereafter published in the official newspaper of said City in accordance with the laws of the State of Idaho; said lands affected and included therein to extend from the said designated portions of said streets and avenues back from such streets and avenues, if platted in blocks, to the center of the blocks, if platted in lots only to the center of the lots; and if not platted, to a distance of 125 feet from the street or avenue upon which said lots or lands face or abutt.

That the streets and avenues of said City and the portions thereof to be improved as named and designated in said Ordinance No. 171, 171, and to which the property included in said Improvement District is abutting, contiguous or fronts on, are as follows, to-wit:-

That portion of Coeurd'Alene Street in the City of St. Maries lying between the South Line of Main Avenue as projected Westward and the North Line of Main Avenue as projected Westward; that por- tion of Coeur d'Alene Street lying between the North Line of Main Avenue as projected Westward and the South Line of Fifteenth Street (which is also a part of the West Line of Coeur d'Alene Street); and that part of Fifteenth Street from the West Line of Coeur d'Alene Street and the North Line of Center Avenue; and that portion of Center Avenue from the West Line of Fifteenth Street to the East Line of Twenty-third Street.

## Section 2

That the general character of the improvements to be made upon the aforesaid portions of said streets and avenues in said: "Local Improvement District No. 4" shall consist of grading them and draining the same by a drainage ditch along and in front of Blocks 40 and 41, Government Townsite to the City of St. Maries, and making such other incidental improvements as may be necessary to permit of said grading.

All of said improvements to be under the supervision of the City Engineer and City Council of the City of St. Maries and in accordance with Ordinance No. 171 of the City of St. Maries; said grading on said streets and avenues to conform to the grades heretofore established by prdinance of said City and according to the profiles thereof now on file in the office of the City Engineer of said City, and all of said improvements shall be made to conform to the plans and specifications of said improvements on file in the office of the City Engineer of said City and adopted and approved by the City Council thereof.

## Section 3

That said improvements shall be and hereby are ordered made and that the cost and expense thereof, including the cost and expense of the improvements in the space formed by the junction of two or more streets and the expense of the improvements in the space formed by the intersection of streets and the expense of the improvements in the space wherein one main street terminates in or crosses another main street and the expense of the improvements in the space opposite alleys and the expense of all the street crossings or crosswalks shall be taxed and assessed upon all the property in said "Local Improvement District No. 4", except the cost and expense of lowering the water mains and pipes where necessary to permit of said grading and the cost and expense required to raise or lower sewer manholes where necessary to pormit of said grading, which last named cost shall be paid by the City of St. Maries.

Where any work for the improvement of any street or avenue mentioned in this ordinance is done or made on one side of the center line of said portion of said streets or avenues, the lots or portions of lots or parcels of land fronting on that side only shall be assessed to cover the expense of said improvements.

## Section 4

The cost and expense of said improvements, except the portion thereof to be paid by the City, shall be assessed upon all the property in said "Local Improvement District No. 4" in proportion to the number of feet of such lands or lots fronting on said portions of said streets or abutting, contiguous or tributary thereto, and in proportion to the benefits derived by said improve- ment, and the expense and cost of said work so assessed on said lands shall become a lien upon said lands, which shall take prece- dence of all other liens and which may be foreclosed in accordance with the provisions of the code of Civil Procedure of the State of Idaho.

Such suit shall be in the name of the City of St. Maries, as plaintiff and in such proceedings where the Court trying the same shall be satisfied that the work has been done or material fur- nished, which, according to the true intent of this Section would be properly chargeable on such lot or lands through or by which the street or avenue improved may pass, a recovery shall be permitted, or a charge enforced to the extent of the proper proportion of the value of the work or material which would be chargeable on such lot or lands notwithstanding any informalities, irregularities or defects in any of the proceedings of this city or through any of its officers.

## Section 5

That upon the passage of this ordinance the street and alley committee, together with the City Engineer shall make out, an assessment roll according to the provisions of this ordinance and of Chapter 5 of the Political Code of the State of Idaho as amended by Chapter 81 of the Session Laws of 1911, rela- ting to the "powers of cities and villages" approved on March 13, 1911, and as further amended by Chapter 97 of the Session Laws of 1913, relating to "powers of cities and villages", approved March 15, 1915, and shall certify the same to the Council of said City of St. Maries. Said assessment roll shall, among other things, contain a list of all property fronting on or abutting, contiguous or tributary to the said portions of the said streets and avenues to be improved and included in said improvement dis- trict, together with the name of the owner or owners of such lots, pieces or parcels of land which are so assessed, if known, the description of said lots, pieces or parcels of land and the amount of assessment levied upon each of such lots, pieces or Ercels of land, which assessment shall be made to cover the entire cost and expense of said improvements of said district, except the portion thereof to be paid by the City as herein provided, in proportion to the number of feet of such lands and lots fronting on or abutting, contiguous or tributary to the por- tions of said streets or avenues to be improved, and in proportion to the benefits derived by said improvements.

## Section 6

Upon receiving the said assessment roll the said City Clerk of said City of St. Maries shall give notice, by three successive publications in the official newspaper of said city, that said assessment roll is on file in his office, stating the date of filing same, and said notice shall state a time at which the City Council will hear and consider objections to said assess- ment roll by parties aggreived by such assessment.

The owner or owners of any property which is assessed in such assessment roll, whether named or not in such roll, may, within ten days from the last publication provided herein, file with the Clerk his or their objections, in writing, to said assessment or any por- tion thereof, which objection must be definite and specific as to the property or piece of property being included in said assessment roll to which said objection is made, as well as to the specific grounds for such objection.

## Section 8

Any person who shall file objections to such assess- ment or re-assessment, as hereinbefore provided, shall have the right to appeal to the District Court of the District of the the State of Idaho, in and for the County of Benewah. Such appeal shall be taken as is provided by Section 9 of Section the Eighth Judicial 2238F of the Revised Codes of the State of Idaho as contained in Chapter 97 of the Session Laws of 1915.

## Section 9

All assessments hereinbefore specified shall be known as "Special Assessments for Improvements" and shall be levied and collected as a separate tax, in addition to the taxes for general revenue purposes, to be placed on the tax roll for collection, subject to the same penalties and collected in the same manner as other city or village taxes.

The cost and expense of the improvements herein provided for, which is chargeable to the abutting, contiguous or approximate property to said portions of said streets and avenues, shall be paid in ten equal annual installments, with interest upon the whole sum so charged as an assessment according to said assess- ment roll at the rate of seven per cent per annum and for such installments, the City Council and Mayor of the City of St. Maries, may issue, in the name of the City of St. Maries, improvement bonds of said "Local Improvement District No. 4". Said improvement bonds of said "Local Improvement District No. 4" shall be issued as herein- after provided and in accordance with Chapter 5 of the Idaho Revised Code and all acts amendatory thereof or of portions thereof.

Such bonâs may, at the discretion of the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, be issued to the contractor or contractors. conducting the said improvements, in payment thereof, or may be sold at not less than their par value, net, and the proceeds thereof paid to said contractor. Such bonds shall not be issued in amount in excess of the contract price or cost of the work of improvement, except that the installment coupons shall include the interest on such installments to maturity thereof.
