An ordinance Creating and Establishing a District Within the Corporate Limits of the Village of St. Maries, Wherein Shall be Confined all Saloons and other Establishments of Whatsoever Nature in which Intoxicating Liquorn are Sold as a Beverage, and Providing that no License of licenses for the Sale of Intoxicating. 

Liquors as a Beverage Shall be Granted Outside of Said Restricted District: 

Whereas, this the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, has been petitioned by a number of the residents, citizens and taxpayers of the Village of St. Maries, to create a restricted district in said village, wherein shall be confined all saloons and other establishments in which intoxicating liquors are sold as a beverage, and 

Whereas, it appears to the said Board of Trustees that the operation of such a restricted district is for the best interest of the Village of St. Maries and the inhabitants thereof,

Now Therefore, Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho,

## Section 1

That there be and there hereby is created in the village of St. Maries a district wherein shall be con- fined all saloons and other establish- ments of whatsoever nature in which in- toxicating liquors are sold as a beverage, and that said district shall include all the territory in the said village of St. Maries-lying north and east of the follow- ing boundary lines, to-wit: Beginning at the intersection of the center line of College avenue and First street, thence west along the center line of College av- enue to its point of intersection with the center line of Second street; thence north along the center line of Second street to its point of intersection with the center line of South Railroad avenue; thence along the center line of South- Railroad avenue in a northwesterly direction to its point of intersection with the center line of Front avenue, thence west along the center line of Front avenue to the intersection of same with the center line of Tenth street; thence north along the center line of Tenth street to the inter- section of same with the north boundary line of the village of St. Maries.

## Section 2

That hereafter no license or licenses for the sale of intoxicating liquors as a beverage at a place or places outside of said district hereby created, shall be granted to any persons whatso- ever

## Section 3

All ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are here- by repealed.

## Section 4

This ordinance shall take effect from and after its passage, appro- val and publication as required by law.

Passed this fifteenth day of February, A. D. 1913, by the Chairman and Board of trustees of the village of St. Maries, Idaho.. 

Approved this 15th day of February A. D. 1913, by the Chairman of the board of trustees of the village of St. Maries, Idaho. 

PERLEY MARTIN, Chairman Board of Trustees
