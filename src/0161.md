An ordinance amending Ordinance No. 132, being an ordinance declaring and fixing the salaries of the officers of the city of St. Maries, Idaho.
Be it ordained, by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That Section 5 of Ordinance No. 132 be amended to read as follows:- That the salary of the Treasurer of the City of St. Maries shall be ($400.) Four Hundred per year.

## Section 2

All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

Passed by the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, this 5th day of April, A. D., 1915.
Approved this 5th day of April, 1915.
Nemy Mukaky, Mayor.
Attest: Spas. R. Schulte, City Clerk.
Published in "The Record" on April 20th, 1915.
