AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING ADDITIONAL RULES AND REGULATIONS FOR THE USE OF THE WATER SUPPLY OF A WATER WORKS AND WATER SUPPLY SYSTEM OF THE CITY OF ST. MARTES, BENEWAH COUNTY, IDAHO; AND

PROVIDING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION OF THE PROVISIONS HEREOF, AND

REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:

## Section 1

That it shall be and it hereby is made unlawful for any person or persons, firm, company, or corporation to use or cause to be used any water supply from the mains of the municipal water works system of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, for the purpose of sprinkling lawns, gardens, side walks or streets, PROVIDED, HOWEVER, that when the water commissioner of the City of St. Maries considers that there is sufficient water supply for the city to permit of the use of city water for sprinkling as aforesaid, he shall be and he hereby is authorized to issue written permits to the users of city water for the sprinkling of lawns, gardens, sidewalks or streets, between the hours of seven and eight P. M., and the sprinkling of lawns, gardens, sidewalks or streets swak with city water shall at all times be unlawful except between said hours, and then only when said person, persons, firm or corporation shall be a golder of said permit.

Said permits when issued by the Water Commissioner shall among other things bear thereon the name of the person, firm or corporation to whom the same is issued, the residence, place of business, buildig or premises whereon the same is to be done, and shall specify the duration of time for which the permit shall be in force, which time shall be in the discretion of the Water Commissioner.

## Section 2

That it shall be unlawful for any person or persons, firm or corporation to maintain, suffer, or permit to remain, in a leaky of defective condition any water pipe, hydrant, reservoir, or water connection of any sort whereby city water may be wasted, or to cause the same to be maintained, suffered, or permitted to remain in such leaky or defective condition for more than twenty four hours after knowledge or discovery of the same.

## Section 3

That it shall be unlawful for any person, or persons firm or corporation to waste or cause to be wasted in any way whatsoever, any water from the water supply works of the City of St.Maries, and it shall like wise be unlawful for any person, firm, or corporation to draw water from any water pipe, hydrant, reservoir, or water connection of any sort for any other purpose than that of domestic use or the use of the business for which the water is furnished, or for fire.

## Section 4

Each and every person, firm, or corporation who shall violate any of the provisions of this ordinance, or any of the provisions of Ordinance No.79 of the city of St. Maries for the violation of which no penalty is fixed therein, shall be deemed guilty of a misdeamnor, and upon conviction thereof for the first offence shall be punished by a fine of One Dollar and costs, and for the second offence shall be punished by a fine of not less than Five Dollars nor more than One Hundred Dollars, or in default of the payment of such fine and costs, imprisoned in the city jail for not less than one day nor more than sixty days.

Passédnby the City Council of the City of St. Maries this 28th day of ly 1915.
