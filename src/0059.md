# Ordinance 59

An ordinance to adopt a seal for the municipal incorporation of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County Idaho.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village St. Maries, Idaho;

## SECTION I

That a seal in circular form with the words "Village of St. Maries," on the upper half of the outer circle, and the words, "Kootenai County, Idaho on the lower half of the outer circle," and the word "Corporate" above, and the word "Seal," below in the center, and the same is hereby made the corporate seal of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho. to be used in all cases in which by the laws of Idaho and the customs of nations, it is necessary to use a seal by a corporation.

## SECTION II

This ordinance shall take effect and be in force after its passage, approval and publication.

Passed this 14th day of September, 1908.

Attest:
J. Turnbull, 
Clerk of the Board.

Perley Martin,
Chairman of the Board.
