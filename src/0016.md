# Ordinance 16

An Ordinance amending Section 34 in Ordinance No 8. 

Be it ordained by the chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maies, that Section 34, of Ordinance No 8, regulating the fees for the Clerk and Marshall of the said Village.

Be and is hereby amended and shall read as follows. 
That all fees due the Marshall for issuing License shall be paid out of the general fund of the Village, said fees not to exceed twenty-five cents to the Marshall, for each license issued. Said Ordinance to
to be inforce after its Publication. All Ordinances or parts of ordinance in conflict with this ordinance are hereby repealed.

Approved Jan 2 1903.

Geoge Hawk
Clerk.

J W Skelton
Chairman
