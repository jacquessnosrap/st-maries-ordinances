An ordinance amending Section 7 of Ordinance No. 132 and amending Ordinance No. 132 by adding Section 7A thereto, which Ordinance No. 132 declares fixes the salaries of the officers of the City of St. Maries, Idaho.

Be it ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, as follows:-

## Section 1

That Section 7 of Ordinance No. 132 be amended to read as follows, to-wit:

Section 7. That the salary of the police officers of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, shall be as follows:- Chief of Police, $85.00 per month; Patrolman, $80.00 per month;

## Section 2

That Ordinance No. 132 be amended by adding thereto section 7 A, as follows:-

Section 7 A. The salary of the Street and Water Commissioner of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, shall be $85.00 per month, and the salary of the assistant street and water commissioner shall be $75.00 per month.

## Section 3

That all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed.

Passed by the City Council this 7th day of June A. D., 1915. Approved by the Mayor of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, this 7th day of June, A. D., 1915.

C. W. Haile Mayor.

Attest: Jas. R. Shinn City Clerk.

Published in "The Record" on June 15, 1915.
