An ordinance levying a tax of One and three-quarters Mills on the Dollar on all property within the Village of St. Maries, taxable according to the laws of the State of Idaho, for the fiscal year ending the first Tuesday in May, 1912, for the purpose of paying the interest on the bonded indebtedness of said village; 

levying a tax of One and one-half Mills on the Dollar on all the taxable property within the said village, for fiscal year ending the first Tuesday of May, 1912, for the purpose of paying an amount into the sinking fund created for the purpose of redeeming the outstanding bonds issued for the new Water Works System of said village:

levying a tax of One and three-quarters Mills on the Dollar on all the taxable property within said village, for the fiscal year ending the first Tuesday in May, 1912, for the payment and redemption of the outstanding warrants of said village;

appropriating all monies arising front all sources to the various purposes for which the same shall be used and designating what monies shall be placed in and appropriated to the "Current Expense Fund."

Be it ordained by the Chairman and the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County. Idaho:

## Section 1

That there be and hereby is, levied upon all property, both real and personal, within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, subject to taxation for the fiscal year ending the first Tuesday in May, 1912, for the purpose of paying the interest on the Municipal Improvement Bonds issued under and pursuant to the provisions of Chapter 12 of Title 13 of Volume 1 of Idaho Revised Codes in order to raise funds to pay for the new Water Works System of said village the sum of One and three-quarters Mills on the Dollar on all property valuations both real and personal, as provided by the laws of Idaho: and all the funds thus raised are hereby appropriated to the fund created for the purpose of paying interest on said bonds-said fund being known as the "Interest Fund\!"
