An Ordinance amending Section One(1) of Ordinance No. 114, of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, being an Ordinance "providing for the regulation and conduct of saloons within the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho ".

Be it ordained by the Mayor and Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, :-

## Section 1.

That Section One(1) of Ordinance No. 114, of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, being entitled "an Ordinance providing for the regulation and conduct of saloons within the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, be and hereby is, amended to read as follows:-

"That it shall be unlawful for any person, persons, firm or corporation to whom a saloon license may hereafter be granted by the Mayor and City Council of said City, to conduct a saloon there- in, to open said saloon or permit same to be opened for business before the hour of six (6) o'clock A.M. in the morning, nor to allow said saloon to remain open for business later than twelve (12) o'clock P.M. in the evening, on any business day, or to permit said saloon to be open for business on any Sunday, or during the hours which it is not permitted to transact business for any purpose, unless it be for the purpose of making some necessary repair or sweeping, mopping, or cleaning said place and that during the time that any person is in there for such purpose no liquor shall be disposed of or taken from the stock of goods therein."

## Section II.

That Section One(1) of said Ordinance No.114, and all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed and rendered of no force or effect.

## Section III.

That this ordinance shall be in effect from and after its approval and publication in one issue of the St. Maries Record, a bi-weekly newspaper of general circulation published in St.Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

Passed its first reading at a regular adjourned meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on the 16th day of March, 1914.

Passed its second reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, at a regular adjourned meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Marios, Idaho, held on the 16th day of March, 1914.

Passed its third and final reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, and duly enacted an ordinance of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, at a regular adjourned meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, hald on March, 16th, 1914.

Approved this 16th day of March, A.D.1914.
