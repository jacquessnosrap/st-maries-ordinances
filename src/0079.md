An ordinance prohibiting the interference with or injury to all water pipes, mains, service pipes, serve boxes, stop boxes and all other parts and appliances, including supply pipes, reservoirs and head works, the water works and water supply system of the village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho; 

Regulating the taking of water therefrom, and preventing pollution of and throwing of any and all substances and matters in any water main, reservuir, supply or other pipe of said system;
