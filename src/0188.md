AN ORDINANCE CONFIRMING AND APPROVING THE CREATION AND ESTABLISHMENT OF LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NO. 3, IN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, IDAHD:

CONFIRMING AND APPROVING THE ASSESSMENT ROLL FOR SAID DISTRICT AS FILED IN THE OFFICE OF THE CITY CLERK, WHICH ASSESSMENT ROLL WAS DULY AND REGULARLY CERTIFIED IN ACCORD ANCE WITH LAW;

APPROVING AND CONFIRMING ALL PROCEEDINGS HAD IN CONNECTION WITH SAID ASSESSMENT ROLL:

PROVIDING FOR THE PAYMENT BY INSTALLMENTS OF THE COST AND EXPENSE OF THE IMPROVEMENTS MADE IN SAID DISTRICT, PROVIDING FOR THE ISSUANCE OF IMPROVEMENT BONDS OF THE DISTRICT:

ASSESSING THE COST OF SAID IMPROVEMENTS AGAINST THE LOTS, BLOCKS AND PARCELS OF LAND IN SAID DISTRICT CONTIGUOUS TO, FRONTING OR ABUTTING ON SAID IMPROVEMENTS, AND DESCRIBING THE MANNER OF COLLECTION AND PAYMENT OF SAID ASSESS- MENTS.

Whereas, the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, has heretofore, pursuant to the requisite preliminary proceedings and ordinance 175 of said City, established local Improvement District No. 3, of said City, for the purpose of improving certain streets and avenues of said city, by grading them, curbing them with concrete curbing, and putting in the necessary cross-walks and make certain other incidental improvements as specified in said ordinance, and,

Whereas the Committee on Streets and Alleys together with the City Engineer, have heretofore in strict conformity with law and the ordinances of said City, made out an assessment roll in and for said Local Improvement District No. 3 fixing the total cost of the improvements therein taxable against private property at $ 25,577.57, assessing said amount against the lots, blocks and parcels of land contiguous to fronting or abutting upon said improvements in said district, said assessments being distributed in strict accordance with law, which assessment roll was filed with the City Clerk on the 30th day of June A. D. 1916, and

Whereas said assessment roll was properly certified by the City Council in strict accordance with law, and

Whereas, after receiving said assessment roll the City Clerk under the direction of the City Council gave notice by three successive publications in the official newspaper of the City of St. Maries, that such assessment roll was on file in his office, giving the date of filing the same, and stating the time at which the City Council would hear and consider objections to said assessment roll by the parties aggrieved by said assess- ment to-wit, on the second day of August, 1916, and stating that the owner or owners of any property which was assessed in such assessment roll, whether named or not in such roll, might within ten days after the last publication of said notice file with the City Clerk his or her objections in writing to the regularity of the proceedings in making such assessment and the correctness of such assessment or of the amount levied on any property, lot or parcel of land, and

Whereas, there were no objections filed with the City Clerk or presented to the City Council against the assessment levied by said Assessment Roll, and

Whereas, after considering said Assessment Roll, the Council ratified, approved and confirmed the same, and

Whereas, it is the opinion of the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, that the cost and expense of the im- provements made in Local Improvement District No. 3, which are chargable to the abutting, adjoining, contiguous or approximate property should be provided for and paid for by installments as specified in the ordinance creating said District No. 3, and

Whereas the Mayor and City Council of the said City desire to issue in the name of the City improvement bonds of the said district to bear date the First day of October A. D. 1916, payable on or before ten years

Now, Therefore, be it Ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That all of the proceedings, matters, and things, neretofore had, taken, and done, in connection with the establishment of Local Improvement District No.3, in the City of St. Maries, Idaho, and the making and constructing therein of the improvements consisting of the improving of cer- tain streets and avenues and parts of streets and avenues in said Improvement District by grading them, curbing them with concrete curbing, and putting in the necessary cross walks and the other incidental improvements particularly specified in Ordinance 175 and the apportionment of the cost of said improvements against the lots, blocks, and parcels of land in said District, be and the same hereby are in all respects approved, ratified, and confirmed.
