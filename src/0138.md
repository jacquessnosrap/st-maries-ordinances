An Ordinance Providing that the Overseer of Streets of the City of St. Maries Shall have Power to Order Repairs to Sidewalks: 

Providing the Manner of Ordering such Repairs, and 

Providing for the Assessment of the Cost of the Same Against the Abutting Property.

Be It Ordained by the Mayor and the Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho.

## Section 1.

That whenever the Overseer of Streets of the Olty of St. Maries shall deem it necessary that any sidewalk be repaired upon any street within sald City he may order the same to be done.

## Section 2.

In case said repairs shall not exceed Ten Dollars ($10.00) in cost he shall give oral or written notice to the owner or owners of the property abutting upon the aidwalk to be repaired, and such owner.or owners shall then commence, or cause to be commenced, such work of re- pairing within twenty-four (24) hours from the time of receipt of such no- tice.

## Section 3.

In case said repairs shall exceed Ten Dollars (10.00) in costs, the Overseer of Streets shall give written notice ordering the re- pairs to be made, to the owner or ownres of the property abutting upon such sidewalk to be repaired, and such owner or owners shall then com. mence work, or cause work to be commenced, upon such repairing in accordance with the said notice, within ten days from the time of the receipt thereof.

## Section 4.

If any property owner having been notified and ordered by the Overseer of Streets as herein pro vided, shall fail or refuse to repair each sidewalk in the manner indicat ed by the said notice and order with- in ten days after receipt of the notice under the condition provided in Sec- tion 3 hereof, and within twenty-four hours after receipt of the notice un- der the condition provided in Section 2 hereof, said Overseer of Streets may proceed to cause said sidewalk to be repaired in such manner as he may deem best.

## Section 7.

Whenever the Overseer. of Streets shall have caused any side. walk to be repaired as provided in Section 4 hereof, the costs of such repair work may be recovered from the owner of the property liable there- for under this ordinance in the man- ner provided in Section 7 of Ordi- nance No. 77 of the City of St. Ma ries. And ail notices provided for herein shall be served according to the provisions of Ordinance No. 77, Section 8, of the City of St. Ma ries.

## Section 8.

Nothing in this ordi nance contained shall be held to abridge the power of the Board of Trustees or the City Council to order repairs to, replacement of, and con struction of sidewalks as provided ty the provisions of Ordinance No. 77 of the City of St. Maries.

## Section 9.

This ordinance shall be in full force and effect from and after its passage, approval, and pab- Mcation in one issue of The Record, of St. Maries, Idaho.

Passed the City Council of the City of St. Maries this 2nd day of June, 1913.

Approved by the Mayor of the Uity of St. Marles this 2nd day of June, 1918.
