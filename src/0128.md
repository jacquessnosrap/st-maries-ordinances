An ordinance to include within and add to the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, territory lying contiguous to and adjacent thereto and hereinafter described, and declaring the same a part of the said Village of St. Maries. 

Whereas, the territory hereinafter described lies contiguous to and adjacent to the Village of St. Maries and the additions and extensions thereof, and 

whereas said territory lies within the corporate boundries of said Village and its additions and extensions,but has never been made a part of said muncipality, and 

Whereas, said territory and land has been, by the owners or proprietors thereof, or by some persons or persons by or with the owner's authority or acquiescence, been laid off into lots or blocks, containing not more than five acres each, 

Now Therefore, Be it ordained by the Chairinan and Board of Trustees of the Villiage of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

## Section 1

That under and by virtue of the laws of the State of Idaho, the following described territory contiguous and adjacent to the Village of St Maries and the additions and extensions thereto, and lying within the corporate boundries of said Village, be and the same hereby is annexed and added to said village and hereby made a part thereof, to-wit;

All that land and territory lying within the following boundries;

beginning at point of intersection of the East line of Coeur d'Alene Avenue and the South line of College Avenue as extended westward and 

thence east along said extended line of Coeur d' Alene Ave. to its point of intersection with the West line of Fourteenth Street;
