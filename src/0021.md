# Ordinance 21

An ordinance relating to the granting of permits for franchises within the corporate limits of the village of St Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, for the construction and operation of Telephone and telegraph lines, electric, gas or other light plants and systems of waterworks to companies, persons, porporations, or associations and providing the manner in which such permits shall be granted.

Be it ordained by the chairman and board of trustees of the village of St Maries, Kootenai County, of Idaho.

## Section 1
Any person company or corporation or association intending to construct or erect, or causing to be constructed or erected within the corporate limits of the village of St Maries, a telephone or telegraph line, electric, gas or other light plant, or any system of waterworks, whereby any part or portion of any highway, bridge street or alley in said corporate limit will be used for the erection of poles, stretching of wires laying underground wires, laying pipes for the purpose of light plants and laying water pipes for a system of waterworks, must first obtain from the board of trustees a permit or franchise.

## Section 2
Any person, company, corporation or association, now owning, controlling or operating any telehone or telegraph line, light plant, or waterwork system within the corporate limits of the village of St Maries, wherein the construction or erection and maintenance or operation of such, any part or portion of the highways, bridges streets or alleys are being used for the purpose of erecting and placing poles, stretching wires either above or below ground, pipes laid for a system or systems of light and waterworks, and where nopermit or franchise for such has been granted by the board of trustees shall within one month after the passage of this ordinance file with the board of trustees an application for such permit or franchise.

## Section 3
No person, company, corporation or association now owning or controlling or operating any telephone or telegraph line, light plants, system of waterworks within the corporate limits of the villiage of St Maries shall further appropriate or make further use of any highway, bridge, street or alley within the corporate limits for the purpose of extending such lines or systems, or make any repairs or alterations to such existing lines and systems, unless first the application for a permit or franchise, required by Section 2 of this ordinance has been filed and finally acted upon by the board of trustees; provided, however, that after an application for permit or franchise has been filed, the board of trustees may grant a permit to make any absolute necessary repairs on such lines and systems as they may deem fit before final action has been taken on such application for permit or franchise.

## Section 4
No permit or franchise contemplated in sections 1 & 2 of this ordinance shall be granted for a longer period than 15 years and shall not be exclusive.

## Section 5
The manner in which any permit or franchise, contemplated in teh foregoing sections of this ordinance shall be granted shall be as follows:
The applicant for a permit or franchise shall file with the board of trustees an application,
The application shall state fully the person company, corporation or association applying for the permit or franchise, the object, purpose, and period for which the permit is asked for; the privelages asked for must be specifically stated, also all highways bridges, streets or alleys sought to be made use of must be fully designated, the mode of how the proposed line or system and the extent of its plants, how built, constructed or to be erected must be fully described, The proposed rates of tabs and charges to patrons and consumers must be stated any concessions in return for permit to be made on the part of the applicant either directly to the village of St Maries or its residents must be stated, and any other information that will fully explain the terms, conditions and purposes under which the permit is asked shall be embodied in such application. After the filiing of such application the board of trustees shall cause an ordinance to be drawn up in conformity with the application and also embodying therein such conditions, terms, regulations and restrictions for the government, operation, and construction of the respective lines or systems as they may deem fit. Such ordinance shall be read three times before final action is taken thereon.
After the filing of an application for a permit or franchise, the applicant shall at his own expense cause to be published a notice that such application has been made, and in such notice shall set forth the purpose, the object for which such permit or franchise is asked the concessions conditions and terms under which such permit is asked.
The highways, bridges, streets and alleys to be appropriated in teh construction fo the lines or systems, the rate of tolls and charges proposed to be charged patrons and consumers. Such notice shall be published in some newspaper published in the village of St Maries for a period of not less than four consecutive weeks and if there is no newspaper published in the village such notice shall be posted in at least four conspicuous places within the village of St Maries.
Upon such notice notice being published, the property owners and taxable inhabitants of the village of St Maries may file with the board of trustees any protest remonstrance or objection against the granting of such permit or franchise, such protest, remonstrance or objection to be in writing and signed; and if the board of trustees find that a majority of the property owners and taxable inhabitants in said village have signed such propest, remonstrance or objection, such permit or franchise shall not be granted.
Should no majority of property owners and taxable inhabitants present protests remonstrances or objections, at the time when the ordinance for a permit or franchise comes up for the third reading, such ordinance shall then come up for final action, and it shall require the three-fourth vote of members elected on the board of trustees to pass such ordinance.

## Section 6
All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict with this ordinance are hereby repealed.

## Section 7
This ordinance shall go into effect upon its passage and after publicatio in the St Maries Courier a newspaper published in the Village of St Maries.

Approved May 11, 1903
