# Ordinance 65 

An ordinance to accept and confirm the dedication of the Streets and alleys in West Addition, Second west Addition, Third West Addition, Railraod Addition and Supplemental To Third West Addition to the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:

## Section 1

That the dedication of the Streets and alleys in the tract of land ~~kursiнниххххd~~ within the limits of said Village and known land designated as West Addition, Second West Addition, Third West Addition, Supplemental to Third West Addition and Railroad addition to said Village plats of which are of record in the office of the County recorder of Kootenai County, Idaho, which dedications were duly made as required by law before said plats were recorded, be and the said dedications of streets and alleys as shown on each of said plats are hereby accepted and confirmed as to any and all streets and alleys shown on said plats.

## Section 2

This ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage and approval and publication as required by law.

Approved this ___[^1] day of April 1909. 

Attest J.S. Turnbull 
Clerk

Perley Martin
Chairman.

[^1]: The date is unrecorded in the original document.
