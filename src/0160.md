An ordinance amending the last paragraph of the preamble of Ordinance No. 159 and 

amending Section 3 of Ordinance No. 159 and

amending Section 4 of Ordinance No. 159 and

amending Section 8 of Ordinance No. 159, which said Ordinance No. 159 is an ordinance comfirming and approving the creation of Local Improvement District No. 2 and

confirming and approving the assessment roll of said district and

confirming and approving all proceedings had in connection with said assessment roll and

providing for the issuance of bonds of said Local Improvement District No. 2.

BE IT RESOLVED, by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho,

## Section 1

That the last paragraph of the preamble to Ordinance No. 159, reading as follows, "Whereas the Mayor and City Council of the said City desire to issue in the name of the City, improvement bonds of the said district to bear date the first day of March, A. D,, 1915, payable on or before ten (10) years" be amended to read as follows:- "Whereas the Mayor and City Council of the said City desire to issue in the name of the City improvement bonds of the said district to bear date the first day of May, A. D., 1915, payable on or before ten (10) years".

## Section 2

That Section 3 of Ordinance No. 159 of the City of St. Maries be amended to read as follows:- "Section 3. That each of the lots, tracts and parcels of land and other property shown and described upon the said assessment roll is hereby declared to be specifically benefitted by said improvement in at least the amount charged against the same and that the assessments appearing against each lot, tract or parcel of land and other property is in proportion to the several assessments bearing upon said work. That there is hereby levied and assessed against each lot, tract and parcel of land and other described property in said roll, the amount finally charged against the same thereon. Said assessment shall be payable in ten (10) equal annual installments, the first of which shall become due and payable on the 24th day of February, A. D., 1916, and each succeeding installment shall become due and payable on the same day in each year thereafter. Provided, however, that all or any portion of said installment may be paid without interest, penalty or cost within the time provided by law prior to the issuance of said bonds. All assessments to bear interest at the rate of seven (7) per cent per annum. Provided, however, that if any property owner desires to redeem his property from liability by paying the entire assessment chargeable against the same after the issuance of the bonds, he may do so by paying all the installments of the assessments which have been levied and also the amount of unlevied installments with interest upon the latter at the rate of seven (7) per cent per annum from the date of issuance of the bonds to the time of maturity of the last installment."

## Section 3

That section 4 of Ordinance No. 159 is hereby amended to read as follows:- "Section 4. That there be issued in the name of the City of St. Maries, improvement district bonds of said Local Improvement District No. 2, which bonds shall bear date on the first day of May, A. D., 1915, and shall bear interest at the rate of seven per cent per annum payable annually on the first day of May in each year. Said interest shall be evidenced by coupons attached to said bonds and said bonds shall be numbered in consecutive numbering commencing with one, and shall be in denominations of Two Hundred ($200.) Dollars each, except the first bond, which may be in such suitable amount as may be required to make the total issue conform with the amount hereby authorized. Said bonds and the coupons thereto attached shall be payable at the office of the City Treasurer of the City of St. Maries. Said bonds shall be counter signed by the Mayor and attested by the City Clerk and counter signed by the City Treasurer of the city of St. Maries, and shall be sealed with the corporate seal of said City; and all interest coupons, may in lieu of the original signatures bear the engraved facsimile signatures of the Mayor, City Clerk and City Treasurer of said City, and when so executed will constitute binding obligations on said district for said interest".

## Section 4

That section 8 of Ordinance No. 159 of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, be amended to read as follows: "Section 8. The Mayor, City Clerk and City Treasurer are hereby authorized and directed to execute and. deliver said bonds to the contractor executing the improvements on or after the first day of April, A. D., 1915, excepting those which may be cancelled by the City Treasurer pursuant to payment made upon his redemption notice given in accordance with law, and the fund of redemptions may be delivered to the contractor."

## Section 5

That the last paragraph of the preamble of Ordinance No. 159 as referred to in Section 1 hereof, and as the same now exists is hereby repealed.

## Section 6

That Section 3 of Ordinance No. 159 as the same now exists, is hereby repealed.

## Section 7

That Section 4 of Ordinance No. 159 as the same now exists, is hereby repealed.

## Section 8

That Section 8 of Ordinance No. 159, as the same now exists, is hereby repealed.

## Section 9

That all ordinances or parts of ordinances in conflict with this ordinance, are in so far as they conflict herewith, hereby repealed.

Passed its first reading at a regular meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on the 1 day of March, A. D., 1915.

Passed its second reading upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, at a regular meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on the 1 day of March, A. D., 1915.

Passed its third and final reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, and duly enacted an ordinance of the City of St. Maries, Idaho at a regular meeting of the City of St. Maries, held on the 1st day of March, A. D. 1915.

Approved this 1st day of March, A. D. 1915.

Attest with the Corporate Seal.

Henry Mayor.

Jas. R. Schulte City Clerk.

Published in "The Record" Mch. 26th 1915.
