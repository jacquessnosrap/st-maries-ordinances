# Ordinance 62

An ordinance to provide for the issueing of coupon bonds by the Village of St. Maries, for the purpose of sale and to apply the proceeds to the construction of Water Works for said Village, to supply the inhabitants thereof with water: provideing for an election of the qualified voters of said Village who are taxpayers therein to determine whether such bonds may be issued: and appointing officers to conduct said election.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries:

## Section 1

That an election of the qualified voters of the Village of St. Maries, who are taxpayers therein, be held on the 20th. day of February A. D. 1909, at Skelton & Warrens Hall in said Village, to determine whether said Village of St. Maries shall issue bonds to the amount of Seventeen Thousand Dollars as provided by law for the purpose selling the same and applying the proceeds to the construction of Water Works for said Village: that the polls shall be open from 8 oclock A. M. until 7 oclock P.M. on said day: and that a notice staging the time of, place of holding and purpose of said election shall be published for thirty days prior to said election in the St. Maries Gazette a newspaper published in said Village.

## Sec. 2

That in the event that Two thirds of said electors voting at said election cast thier ballots in favor of the issuing of said bonds, there shall be issued by said Village of St. Maries Seventeen Coupon bonds of the denomination of one thousand dollars each, numbered from one to seventeen inclusive, payable twenty years from date with interest coupons attached, bearing interest at a rate not to exceed six percent, payable semiannually, payable to purchaser or bearer, interest and principal payable at the office of the Village treasurer of said Village.

## Sec. 3

That said bonds shall be sold to the highest bidder as provided by law and the poceeds of such sale shall be used to purchase materials, right of way for pipe line and reservoir site or sites and pay for labor and for any other purpose necessary to construct a system of water works to supply the inhabitants of said Village with water which is hereby declared to be the purpose for which said bonds are to be issued.

## Sec. 4.

That there shall be levied annually and collected, upon the taxble property within said Village a sum sufficient to pay the interest on said bonds which shall be kept as a separate fund and be known as the interest fund which shall be used for the purpose of paying said interest only: That there shall also be levied annually and collected, upon the taxable property within said Village a sum to be deposited in some safe bank, as a sinking fund which said annual levies shall be sufficient to pay the principal of said bonds in twenty years, and which said fund shall be used for said purpose.

## Sec. 5.

That in case of the sale of said bonds that the said Village of St. Maries shall erect, construct, maintain and operate a water works system for said Village for the purpose of furnishing pure and wholesome water to the inhabitants thereof and for the purpose of protecting the property within said Village from destruction by fire and that the proceeds of the sale of said bonds be used to purchase all things necessary to that end.

## Sec. 6.

That *J N Skelton Leove Pererovot S. M. Puabbuilt*[^1] are hereby appointed as judges and *Z A Wade and Z A Johnston and Z N E Russell* are hereby appointed as clerks of said election and empowered to conduct the same.

## Sec. 7

This ordinance shall take effect and be inforce from and after its passage and publication in one issue of the St. Maries Gazette a newspaper published in said Village.

Passed & approved the 6th day of Jan, 1909

Parley Martin,
chairman

Attest
J. S. hornbull
Clerk
