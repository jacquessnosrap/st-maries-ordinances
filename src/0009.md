# Ordinance 9 

An Ordinance for the protection of side walks, forbidding injuries to the same and the obstruction thereof, and providing penalties for violations.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries. 

## Section 1
That any person or persons who shall wilfully injure or destroy any of the sidewalks constructed or which shall hereafter beconstructed in front of any premises in this Village, or shall place obstructions thereof, or shall tear up or remove any portion of the same, without having first obtained permission of the Board of Trustees, shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor, and upon conviction, shall be fined in any sum not to exced TWENTY FIVE DOLLARS, and in default of payment thereof shall be confined in the Village jail one day for each two dollars of said fine. 

## Section 2
That any person or persons who shall drive or lead any, team, wagon or other veichle drawn by a horse or other animal along or across any of the sidewalks of this Village, or shall ride, lead or drive any horse along said sidewalks, shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor, and upon conviction thereof shall be fined in any amount not to exceed TWENTY FIVE DOLLARS, and in default of payment there of shall be confined in the Village jail one day for each two dollars of said fine. 

Approved November 6, 1902. 

Attest. 
George Hawk 
Clerk. 

JW Skelton
Chairman.
