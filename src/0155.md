AN ORDINANCE CREATING A DISTRICT WITHIN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, KOOTELIAI COUNTY, IDAHO, IN WHICH NO BUILDING, OR ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING, SHALL BE CONSTRUCTED EXCEPT OF BRICK, STONE, CONCRETE OR OTHER INCOMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL, WITH FIRE PROOF ROOF:

PRESCRIBING THE LIMITS OF SAID DISTRICT:

PROVIDING THAT NO SPECIAL PERMITS SHALL BE GIVEN FOR THE ERECTION ON ANY BUILDING OR ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING OF COMBUTIABLE MATERIAL WITHIN SUCH DISTRICT:

DECLARING ANY BUILDING OR ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING HEREATTER CONSTRUCTED WITHIN SAID DISTRICT OF COMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL TO BE A NUISANCE:

PROVIDING THAT THE CITY COUNCIL MAY REMOVE OR DEMOLISH, OR CAUSE TO BE REMOVED OR DEMOLISHED ANY BUILDING OR ANY ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING HEREAFTER ERECTED IN SAID DISTRICT OF COMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL IN VIOLATION OF THIS ORDINANCE AND RECOVER THE COSTS THEREON FROM THE OWNER OF SUCH BUILDING AND 

REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDIANANCES IN CONFLICT WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ORDIANANCE.

BE IN ORDAINED, by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

## Section 1.

That there be and there hereby is created a district within the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, within which no buildings, or additions to buildings shall hereafter be constructed except of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material, with fire proof roof.

## Section 2.

That said District shall include all the territory within the following described limits and boundaries, towit: Commencing at the point of intersection of the east and west alley in block 23 of Railroad addition to St. Maries with the east l'ine of 10th street; Thence east along said alley through blocks 23, 22 and 21 of Railroad addition to the intersection of same with the west line of seventh (7) street; thence south along the west line of seventh (7) street to the southeast corner of lot one (1) of block eight (8) of West addition; thence west along the south line of lots one (1), two (2), three (3) and four (4) of said block eight (8) to the east line of eight (8) street; thence south along the east line of eight (8) street to the east and west alleey between lots five (5) and six (6) of said Block eight (8), west addition; thence west across eight (8) street and along the east and west alley in blocks one (1) and eight (8) of 2nd West Addition, to the East line of tenth (10) street; thence north along said east line of tenth (10) street to the point of beginning.

## Section 3.

That from and after the passage, approval and pub- lication of this ordinance it shall be unlawful for any person, or persons, corporation or corporations to erect or construct any building or addition to any building, within the above described district, except such building or addition as the case may be, be constructed of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material, with fire proof roof.

## Section 4.

That no special permit shall be given or granted for the erection of any building, or addition to any building, within said district, by the Mayor or City Council or any other officer or agrent of said City of St. Maries, of combustible mater- ial.

## Section 5.

That any building, or addition to any building, hereafter constructed within said district, of combustible material, in violation of this ordinance, shall be and the same hereby is declared to be a nuisance and the City Council may remove or demolish, or cause to be removed or demolished any such building or addition, erected within said district, of combustible material, in violation of this ordianance and the costs thereof may be recovered from the owner of such building or addition by action in the Police Court of said City, or in any other Court of competent jurisdiction.

## Section 6.

That all ordianances and parts of ordinances in conflict with the provisions of this ordinance are hereby repealed.
