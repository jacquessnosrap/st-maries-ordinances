An Ordinance amending Ordinance No. 158 of the City of St. Maries, entitled: AN ORDINANCE PROHIBITING THE DRAGGING OF POLES, LOGS, TIES, DRAG RACKS, LOGGING DRAYS, AND LIKE VEHICLES UPON GRADED AND OTHERWISE IMPROVED STREETS OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES; PROHIBITING THE BACKING OF VEHICLES INTO ANY OF THE CURBS OF ANY OF THE STREETS OF ST. MARIES AND PROHIBITING ANY VEHICLE FROM BEING DRIVEN OR OTHERWISE PROPELLED ACROSS OR UPON ANY CURB OR ACROSS OR UPON ANY GUTTER WITHIN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES OTHER THAN AT THE PLACES PROVIDED FOR SUCH CROSSING; MAKING ANY OF SAID ACTS A MISDEMEANOR AND PROVIDING A PENALTY THEREFOR; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH; by adding thereto a section 4a and 4b.

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and Common Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:-

## Section 1

That Ordinance No. 158 of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, entitled: AN ORDINANCE PROHIBITING THE DRAGGING OF POLES, LOGS, TIES, DRAG RACKS, LOGGING DRAYS, AND LIKE VEHICLES UPON GRADED AND OTHERWISE IMPROVED STREETS OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES; PROHIBITING THE BACKING OF VEHICLES INTO ANY OF THE CURBS OF ANY OF THE STREETS OF ST. MARIES AND PROHIBITING ANY VEHICLE FROM BEING DRIVEN OR OTHERWISE PROPELLED ACROSS OR UPON ANY CURB OR ACROSS OR UPON ANY GUTTER WITHIN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES OTHER THAN AT THE PLACES PROVIDED FOR SUCH CROSSING; MAKING ANY OF SAID ACTS A MISDEMEANOR AND PROVIDING A PENALTY THEREFOR; AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES OR PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH, be and the same hereby is amended by adding to said Ordinance a section to be known as Section 4a and reading as follows:-

Section 4a. That it be and hereby is prohibited and made unlawful for any person, firm or corporation to drive, drag, pull, push and otherwise propell across or upon any side walk or side walks of any character whatsoever within the City of St. Maries, any wagon or other vehicle of any character whatsoever at a point or place other than a point or place provided for such crossing; AND PROVIDED that no point or place shall be considered as such a crossing point or place in any side walk unless such point or place of crossing be constructed under the supervision of the Street Commissioner or in a manner approved by him.

## Section 2

That said Ordinance No. 158 be amended by adding to it Section 4b, reading as follows:-

Section 4b. That it be and hereby is prohibited and made unlawful for any person or persons to coast or slide with a sled or any other vehicle or instrument or appliance upon any side walk or side walks within the City of St. Maries, or the corporate limits thereof.

## Section 3

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval and publication.

Presented by the City Council at regular meeting thereof held on the 7th day of February 1916. Approved this 7th day of February 1916.

O.E. Hailey, Mayor
Attest: C.R. Schulte, City Clerk
