An Ordinance Declaring And Fixing The Salaries of the officers of the City of St. Maries, Idaho. 

Whereas, the Village of St. Maries has in a manner provided by law, been duly and properly organized into a city of the second class under the laws of the State, of Idaho, and 

whereas at the next enisung general election to be held in said municipality said Village will have all the power and duties of a city of the second class, and 

Whereas, the officers to be elected at said next ensuing general municipal election will be such officers as are provided for cities of the second class, and 

Whereas, by the laws of the State of Idaho, the salaries of officers of cities of the second class shall be fixed by ordinance. 

Now Therefore, Be It Ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Villege of St. Maries, Idano,

## Section 1

That the salary of the mayor of the City of St Maries, Idaho shall be One Hundred Dollars per year.

## Section 2

That the salary of the clerk and ex-officia police judge of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, shall be Seventy-five Dollars per month.

## Section 3

That the salary of the members of the city council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, shall be Twenty- four Dollars per year.

## Section 4

That the salary of the city engineer of the City of St Maries, Idaho shall be a reasonable value of his services:

## Section 5

That the salary of the treasurer of the City of St. Maries shall be Two Hundred, Dollars per year

## Section 6

That the salary of the City\! attorney of the City of St. Maries, Idaho shall te Twenty-five Dollars per month, provided that in the case of bond issues and all suits and action in which paid municipality shall be plaintiff or defendant or party thereto, he shall be paid the reasonable value of his services.

## Section 7

That the salary of the police officers of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, shall be as follows:-Chief of Police, One Hundred Dollars per month.

## Section 8

That the salaries of the re- spective officers above named shall be paid at the end of each fiscal month, in proportion to the time during which the respective office is occupied,

## Section 9

All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby, repealed.

## Section 10

This ordinance shall be in effect after its passage approval;

and publication and after the felection and qualification of the city officers herein before set forth.

Passed by the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, this 8th day of March, A. D., 1913.

Approved by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the Village of St Maries, Idaho, this 8th day of March A. D., 1913.
