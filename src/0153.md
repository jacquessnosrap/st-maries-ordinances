An ordinance entitled "The Annual Appropriation Bill" for the fiscal year commencing on the 1st Tuesday in May, 1914, appropriating the total sum of Nineteen Thousand Eight Hundred Fifty Dollars ($19,850) to defray the necessary expenses and liabilities of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, for said fiscal year, specifying the object and purposes for which such appropriations are made, and the amount appropriated for each object milis on each or purpose; and levying a tax of 12.44 dollar of valuation on all taxable property, real and personal, within the corporate limits of the City of St. Maries. Be it ordained, by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:-

## Section 1 

That the sum of Nineteen Thousand Eight. Hundred Fifty Dollars ($19,850) be appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources and from the tax levy hereinafter provided for, to defray the necessary expenses and liabilities of the City of St. Maries, for the fiscal year commencing on the 1st Tuesday in May, 1914, and that said sum be appropriated to the various pur- poses and objects in the amounts hereinafter provided.

## Section 2

That there be and there is hereby, appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Six Thousand Dollars ($6,000) to be known as the Salary Fund, to be used for the purpose of paying the salaries of the city officers.

## Section 3

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from the 12.44 mill tax levy hereby provided, and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Eight Hundred Fifty Dollars ($850), to be known and designated as the Sinking Fund, to be used for the purpose of redeeming all outstanding bonded indebtedness of said city upon its water works system as provided by Ordinance No. 70 of said City.

## Section 4

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from said mill tax levy herein provided, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Eight Hundred Fifty Dollars ($850) to be known as the Interest Fund, for the purpose of pay- ing the interest upon the outstanding bonded indebtedness of said city, upon its water works system as provided in Ordinance No. 70 of said City.

## Section 5

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out os all moneys received from all sources of said city during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of One Thousand Dollars ($1,000) to be known and designated as the Water Works lund, to be used for the purpose of extending and maintaining the water works system of said city.

## Section 6

That therepe and there hereby is appropriated out of all moneys received, from all sourcesby said city, during said year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Twenty-three Hundred Dollars ($2,300) to be known as the Light Fund, to be used for the purpose of lighting the streets, avenues, alleys and public buildings of said city.

## Section 7

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources by said city, during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Three Hundred Dollars ($300) to be known as the Fire Fund, to be used for fire protection purposes in said city.

## Section 8

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of the annual tax levy herein provided, the sum of Two Hundred Dollars ($200) to be known as the Library Fund, to be used for the purpose of maintaining a public library in said city and paying the expenses thereof.

## Section 9

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources by said city during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000), to be known as the Current Expense Fund, to be used for the pur ose of defraying the current ex- penses of said city during said year.

## Section 10

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from the annual tax levy herein pro- vided, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Four Thousand Two Hundred Dollars ($4,200) to be known as the Warrant Redemption Fund, to be used for the purpose of paying the outstanding Warrant indebtedness of said city on account of warrants issued by said city and now outstanding.

## Section 11

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from all s urces by said city during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of Two Thousand Dollars ($2,000) to be known as the Public Improvement Pund, to be used for the pur ose of paying for public improvements in said city during said year.

## Section 12

That there be and there is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources by said city during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of One Hundred Fifty Dollars ($150) to be known as the Cemetary Fund, to be used for the purpose of maintaining and improving Woodlawn Cemetary now owned by said city.

## Section 13

That there be and there is hereby levied upon all the taxable property, both real and personal, within the corporate limits of the said city of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, subject to taxation for the fiscal year commencing the first Tuesday in May, 1914, the sum of 12.44 mills on each one dollar of valuation.

## Section 14

That it shall be the duty of said Treasurer to distribute the moneys received by said city during said year from said annual tax levy and from all other sources, and appor- tion the same to the various funds ad herein provided for and to keep a separate acc unt of the receipts credited to each fund and expended therefrom.

## Section 15

That all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict with this ordinance are hereby repealed.

## Section 16

That this ordinance shall take effect and be in force upon its passage, approval and publication.

Passed its first reading at a special meeting of the city Council of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, State of Idaho, held on the 20th day of June, 1914, upon which a yea and ney vote was taken.

Passed its second reading upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, at a special meeting of the City Council held on the 20th day of June, 1914.

Passed its thierd and final reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and dnay vote was taken and duly enacted an ordinance of the City of St. Maries, at a special meeting of the City Council held on the 20th day of June, 1914.
