An ordinance providing for and regulating the construction of sidewalks within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, providing the method of ordering such construction: providing for the assessment of the cost of same against the property holder abutting thereon or adjacent thereto and providing the kind of side walks to be constructed.

## Section 1 
That all sidewalks hereafter ordered to be built shall be built upon the grades heretofore or hereafter established: provided however that the Board of Trustees may order temporary side walks to conform to the surface of the Ground where the streets have not been graded.

Whenever the Board of Trustees shall deem it necessary that any sidewalk shall be constructed, replaced or repaired upon any street within said Village, they shall order the same done by resolution, order or motion spread upon their minutes; provided that if any sidewalk be ordered of a different mode of construction of dimensions than heretofore described then and in that event the said resolution, order or notion shall state specifically the kind of side walk and mode of construction thereof.
