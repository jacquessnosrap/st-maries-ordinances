# Ordinance 49

An ordinance prohibiting the placeng of obstructions on any of the treets and avenues in the village of St. Maries, Idaho, and providing penalty for any violation thereof.
BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CHAIRMAN AND BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE VILLAGE OF ST. MARIES, IDAHO:

## Section 1
That it shall be unlawful for any person, persons,company or corporation to obstruct any of the streets and avenues in the corporate limits of the village of St. Maries, Idaho by placing and leaving thereon any wood, lumber, logs, poles, wagons, machinery and such other articles and matter which will have a tendancy to obstruct or blockade such streets and avenues; PROVIDED, that nothing herein contained shall prevent the use of so much of the streets and avenues, as may be necessary, for the piling of lumber and materials, to be used in the actual construction of buildings in course of construction.

## Section 2
That any person, persons, company or corporation violating Section I of this ordinance, shall, upon conviction therof, be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor and subject to a fine of not less than five dollars and not more than fifty dollars, and cost of prosecution.

## Section 3
That this ordinance shall take effect and be in force after its passage, approval and publication.

PASSED by the Board and APPROVED by the CHAIRMAN this 6th day of \[unreadable\], 1907.
