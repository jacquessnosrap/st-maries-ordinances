# Ordinance 11

An Ordinance fixing the salaries of the officers of the Village of St Maries, Kootenai County, State of Idaho, for the Fiscal year commencing on November 15, 1902.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County State of Idaho. That the salaries of the Officers of said Village of St. Maries for the remainder of the fiscal year of 1902 and 1903, commencing November 10, 1902, shall be as follows. 

Clerk $1 per meeting.

Attorney

Marshall $5 per month

Treasurer $5 per year

By order of the Board of Trustees
Aproved. Nov. 6 1902

Attest.
George Hawk
Clerk.

J W Skelton
Chairman
