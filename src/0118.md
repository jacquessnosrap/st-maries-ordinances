An ordinance amending section 18 of ordinance No. 111, of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, being entitled "An ordinance Creating Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 3, in the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, defining the boundaries thereof and providing for the construction of a sewerage system therein, providing for the appointment of a sewer committee and defining and regulating their powers and duties; 

providing for the levying of a special assessment for the sewerage improvement and the expenses connected therewith and providing for the issuing of bonds therefor;

providing for the payment of said assessment in ten (10) equal annual installments and for the collection thereof;

prescribing the duties of the village clerk and village treasurer in connection with the collection thereof;

providing for the redemption of said bonds;

providing for the assessment of the property in said Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 3, and for the preparation of an assessment roll and for the hearing of protests against the same and providing for the repealing of all ordinances in conflict therewith, and amending and repealing said section 18 of said ordinance No. 111 of the Village of St. Maries, and all other ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith. 

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:

## Section 1

That section 18 of ordinance No. 111 of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, being entitled "An ordinance creating Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 3 in the village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, defining the boundaries thereof and providing for the construction of a sewerage system therein; providing for the appointment of a sewer committee and defining and regulating their powers and duties; providing for the levying of a special-assessment for the sewerage improvement and the expenses connected therewith and providing for the issuing of bonds therefor; providing for the payment of said assessment in ten (10) equal annual installments and for the collection thereof; prescribing the duties of the village clerk and village treasurer in connection with the collection thereof; providing for the redemption of said bonds; providing for the assessment of property in said Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 3. and for the preparation of an assessment roll and for the hearing of protests against the same and providing for the repealing of all ordinances in conflict herewith, be amended to read as follows:

"The said bonds shall be payable on or before the 11th day of March, 1922, and shall bear interest at the rate of 8 per cent per annum, which said interest shall be payable annually on the 11th day of March in each year at the office of the village treasurer of said village, or at some banking house in New York City, New York at the option of the purchaser thereof. Said bonds shall be issued in denominations of $250 each and shall be numbered from one upward consecutively, and each bond and coupon shall be signed by the Chairman of the board of Trustees and attested by the clerk of the village, provided, however, that said coupon may, in lieu of being so signed, have printed thereon the facsimile of the signature of each officer and each bond shall have the seal of the village affixed thereto and shall refer to the improvement for the payment of which the same shall be issued."

## Section 2

Section 18 of said ordinance No. 111 of the village of St. Maries, and all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed and rendered of no further force and effect.

## Section 3

This ordinance shall be in full force and effect from and after its passage, approval and publication in one issue of the St. Maries Gazette, a weekly newspaper of general circulation published in the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

Passed the Board of Trustees, and approved this 27th day of February, A. D. 1912.
O. E. Hailey, Chairman Board Trustees.
Attest: Chas. R. Schulte, Village Clerk
