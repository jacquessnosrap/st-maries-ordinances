An Ordinance Changing the Name of Front Avenue of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, to Main Avenue, and 

Changing the Name of Main Avenue thereof to Jefferson Avenue, and 

Changing the Name of Coeur d'Alene Avenue Thereof to Coeur d'Alene Street, and 

Changing the Names of all the Streets and Avenues in the Government Townsite Addition to the Village of St. Maries, and Directing the Recording Hereof.

Whereas it appears necessary for the welfare and good government of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, that the names of certain streets and avenues therein be changed, now, therefore,

Be It Ordained, by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries:-

## Section 1.

That from and after the passage, approval, and publication of this ordinance, that certain avenue known as Front Avenue of the village of St. Maries, as shown and laid out upon the official maps and plats of the village of St. Maries, Idaho, and of the various additions thereto, shall be known as Main avenue, and that the name of said avenue be and the same hereby is changed from that of Front avenue to Main avenue

## Section 2.

That from and after the passage, approval; and publication of this ordinance, that certain avenue known as Main avenue of the village of St. Maries; as shown and laid out upon the official plat and maps of the village of St. Maries; Idaho, and the various additions thereto, shall be known as Jefferson avenue, and that the name of said avenue be and the same hereby is changed from that of Main avenue to Jefferson avenue.

Coeur d'Alene street.
