# Ordinance 53 

Ordnance to amend section 5 of ordinance No 43 passed and approved on the 1st day of July, 1907.

BE IT ORDAINED BY THE CHAIRMAN AND BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE VILLAGE OF ST. MARIES:

## Section 1
That section 5 of Ordinance No.43, passed and approved on the Ist day of July, 1907, be and the same is hereby amended to read as follows:

Section 5. The said F.A.Johnston, his successors, heirs and assigns shall within sixty days after the passage of this ordinance file with the Village Clerk of said village a written acceptance of said ordimance and its conditions, and any failure to file such writen acceptance shall render this ordinance void and of no effect.

## Section 2
That this ordinance shall take effect and be in force afetr its passage, approval and publication.

Passed and approved this day 2nd of ~~October~~ December 1907.

Attest:
Thomas C White,
Village Clerk.

Penby Martin
Chairman
