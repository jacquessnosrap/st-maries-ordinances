An ordinance authorizing the Coeur d'Alene & St. Joe Transportation Company, Limited, to construct and maintain a levee or dock and warehouse in aid over the St. Joe River, at the foot of Tenth Street of the City of St. Maries, and regulating the use thereof.

Be it ordained, by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:

## Section 1

That the Coeur d'Alene & St. Joe Transportation, Company, Limited, a corporation, be and the same hereby is authorized to construct and maintain a levee or dock and a warehouse for landing and depot purposes and for storing freight, in and over that portion of the St. Joe River contiguous and adjacent to the East twenty (20) feet of the foot of Tenth Street in the City of St. Maries between a projection of the East Boundary Line of said street and a projection of a line parallel thereto and twenty (20) feet West thereof.

## Section 2

That the license and authority hereby granted to said company is granted upon the following conditions and subject to the following regulations:
(a) That within ninety (90) days after the passage and approval of this ordinance, said company shall proceed to construct and build a good and substantial levee or dock in that portion of the St. Joe River above described and designated to be used as a landing place for said company's boats. Provided, however, that, subject to the prior and superior right of said company thereto, said dock shall be free and open to the public as a landing place for private launches and boats, but shall not be open for use by any other common carrier of goods or passengers except upon such terms as said company may designate. PROVIDED FURTHER, HOWEVER, That nothing herein shall be construed to prohibit the City of St. Maries from granting a like privilege to any other common carrier on the remaining portion of the foot of said Tenth Street or any other place in said City of St. Maries.
(b) The license and authority herein granted shall continue so long as said company shall maintain regular daily boat service between said City of St. Maries and other points on the St. Joe River and Lake Coeur d'Alene as a common carrier of goods and passengers, unless sooner revoked as herein provided.
(c) Said company shall use said levee or dock as a regular stopping place for all of its regularly scheduled boats and its abandonment thereof as a regular stopping place for said boats for a period of eight consecutive months shall revoke the license and authority herein granted.
(d) Said company shall keep said levee or dock in a safe and suitable condition at all times and its failure to do so after thirty (30) days written notice by said City of St. Maries, shall revoke the license and authority herein granted.
(e) The license and authority herein granted does not extend to any part of Tenth Street and shall in no way interfere with the use of said Tenth Street as a public street and highway.
(f) Whenever the City of St. Maries shall desire to take over and maintain said levee or dock as a public levee and dock it may revoke the license and authority herein granted, upon paying to said company the reasonable value of said dock and levee in the condition it shall then be in.
(g) The license and authority herein granted shall take effect and be in force from and after the acceptance thereof by said company, which acceptance shall be in writing and attested by the seal of said company and shall be filed with the Clerk of said City within ninety (90) days from the passage and approval of this ordinance. Provided, however, that the construction of the levee or dock as herein provided by said company shall constitute and be construed as an acceptance of all of the terms of this ordinance.
(h) Upon the expiration of the license and authority herein granted, or upon its revocation for any of the reasons herein set forth, said levee or dock and said warehouse, if constructed, shall revert to and become the property of said City of St. Maries.

## Section 3

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval and publication.
