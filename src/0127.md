An ordinance for the purpose of providing for the burial of the dead within the village of St. Maries. Kootenai Co., Idaho, 

providing where, and in what manner, all dead persons shall be buried within any cemetery within the said village, or under its control, 

providing for the appointment of a superintendent to take charge of the cemeteries under the control of said village and defining the duties of the said superintendent, and 

providing that no person shall be buried within said village, or in any cemetery under its control, except as provided in this ordinance and 

providing for the sale of cemetery lots and granting of title thereto and 

providing for the adornment and ornamentation of the cemeteries, or graves therein, under the control of said village and 

providing further for such rules and regulations for the conduct and control of said cemeteries as shall be passed and adopted by the chairman and board of trustees of said villuge and

providing a penalty for the violation of any of the 

provisions of this ordinance, 

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho.

## Section 1

That it shall be unlawful to bury any dead person within the cor- porate limits of the Village of St. Maries, or within any cemetery under control of said village, except as hereinafter pro- vided.

## Section 2

Upon the passage and adoption of this ordinance the Board of Trustees shall elect a Super intendent of Cemeteries who shall have general charge of all cemeteries under the control of the Village of St. Maries.

## Section 3

Any person desiring to bury any dead person within said vil- lage or within any cemetery under the control of said village, shall first obtain a permit from the superintendent, of Cemeteries, and shall burý such dead person under such rules and regulations as shall be prescribed and adopted by the board of trustees of said village, and under direction of said Superintendent of Cemeteries.

## Section 4

It shall be unlawful to plant any trees, erect any monuments or tombstones or divert the use of any cemetery lot, erect any ience or palings within said cemeteries, of any kind or na- ture or without the consent of the Superin-j tendent of, Cemeteries, or to adorn' in any manner, whatever said cemeteries without such consent.

## Section 5

The Superintendent of Cemeteries. shall have power in addi- tion to the powers: granted to him by the rules and regulations to be hereafter adopted to fix and determine, by and with the consent of the Board of Trus- tees, the size of cemetery lots (within said cemetery and to fix and determine the price at which said lots are to be sold, and to sell the same.

## Section 6

Upon the sale of any com- etery lot or lots, and the payment of the entire purchase price therefor, it shall be the duty of the Chairman of the Board of Trustees, to issue to said pur- chaser a certificate countersigned by the clerk under the seal of said village. specifying that the person to whom the same is issued is the owner of the lot or lots, described therein, as laid down on the maps or plats of said cemeteries for the purpose of interment, which said certificate shall be signed by said chair- man and shall vest in said purchaser, such title as is now prescribed by law. for such purpose, subject to the control and regulation by the board of trustees as is now provided by statute

## Section 7

That the Superintendent of Cemeteries shall act under such rules and regulations as the chairman and the board of trustees shall from time to time adopt, as to all matters not pro- vided for in this ordinance, which said rules, and regulations, when adopted, shall be printed and delivered to said Superintendent of Cemeteries, in such number as will enable him to deliver a copy thereof to any person interested, and upon the change or alteration, thereof, new copies shall likewise be printed and delivered to said Superin-, tendent, which said rules and regulations as adopted shall be kept on file with the clerk of said village, and upon the change or alteration thereof a new copy shall be filed with said clerk, and said; rules and regulations shall when adopt- ed be and the same hereby are declared to be a part of this ordinance.

## Secaion 8

Any person violeting any of the provisions of this ordinance or any rule adopted by the chairman and board of trustees pursuant to this ordi- nance for the regulation and control of said cemeteries shall be deemed guilty of a inisdemeaner and shall be fined in a sum not less than five dollars, and not more than one hundred dollars, and in case of failure to pay said fine the Court shall have power to order that the same. be paid by labor upon the streets alleys parks, public highways, or other public works under the control of the said village at the rate of two dollars per day, until such fine and the costs of prosecution are fully paid and discharged.

## Section 9

This ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage approv- al and publication
