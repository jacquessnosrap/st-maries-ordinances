An ordinance creating the office of water, sewer, and light commissioner; and 

refining the duties of that officer: and 

determining and fixing the salary of that officer, and also determining and fixing the salary of the overseer of streets; and 

repealing sections one. two and three of ordinance No. 99. 

Be it ordained by the mayor and the council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho.

## Section 1

That the office of water, sewer and light commissioner, be, and the same hereby is created.

## Section 2

That the water, sewer, and light commissioner shall be under and subject to the direct control of the council, and he shall have in addition to any general or special duties that the council or the mayor shall from time to time cause to be devolved upon that officer, the fol- lowing duties, towit:-General super- vision of the water, sewer, and lighting systems of the city; to make or cause to be made, all needful repairs and improv ements to the water system of the city, as well as to the sewer system, and to the lighting systems in so far as it shall apply to the satety, comfort, and gener- al welfare of the city and the inhabi- tants thereof, and to make the necessary taps and connections to the sewer and and water systems, subject at all times so the directions of the council, and the mayor.

To make a monthly report to the council, to be oral or written, and of such nature and kind as the council and mayor in the exercise of their discretion may determine, and which report is to be presented to the council at their reg- ular monthly meeting. Such report should show the general condition of the water, sewer and light systems, and be accompanied by the recommendations of the commissioners.

## Section 3

That the salary of the water, sew- and light commissioner shall be Seventy Five Dollars per month, payable by war- rant at the end of each fiscal month.

## Section 4

That the salary of the overseer of the streets shall be Seventy Five Dollars per month payable by warrant at the end of each fiscal month.

## Section 5

That section one, two and three of Ordinance No. 99, entitled "A'n or li nance determining and fixing the salar. ies of the various officers of the village of St. Maries; creating the office of the street and water commissioner and defining the duties of that officer, and repealing Ordinance No. 68, be, and the same hereby are repealed and made of no force or effect.

## Section 6

That this ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval and publication.


Passed by council of the city of St Maries this 6th day of May A.D. 1913.

Approved by the mayor of the City of St. Maries this 6th day of May A.D. 1913.

Henry L. Mulahy
Mayor of the City of St Maries
