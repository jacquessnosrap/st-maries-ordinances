An ordinance requiring and directing all the owners of property in that portion of the City of St. Maries west of Coeur d' Alene Street and south of the St. Joe River, commonly known as the Government Townsite, to cut and remove all trees, timber and underbrush from the portions of the streets and alleys in said Government Townsite, upon which their respective property abutts, providing a time within which same must be completed and providing that in the event said owners, or any of them shall fail to so cut and remove said trees, timber and und er brush within the time provided, that same shall be done by the Street Commissioner or other authorized person, and the costs thereof assessed against the owner or owners of the property abutting on the portions so cleared.

WHEREAS, that portion of the City of St. Maries west of Coeur d' Alene Street and south of the St. Joe River, commonly known as the Government Townsite, is heavily covered with trees, timber and underbrush and the streets, avenues and alleys thereof are likewise heavily covered with trees, timber and underbrush, and

WHEREAS, the property of the residents in said Government Town- site is in eminent danger of damage by forest fires during the dry seasons of the year by reason of sai trees, timber and underbrush.

WHEREAS, said danger of fires will be greatly reduced by the cutting and removing of said trees, timber and underbrush from the streets, avenues and alleys of said Government Townsite.

NOW WHEREFORE, be it ordained by the Mayor ad City Council of the City of St. Maries:

## Section 1.

That each and every owner of property in that por- tion of the City of St. Maries located west of Coeur d' Alene Street and south of the St. Joe River, commonly known as the Government Townsite, be and they hereby are required and directed to cut and remove all trees, timber and underbrush now standing, lying or being upon the portion or portions of the streets, avenues or alleys, in said Government Townsite, upon which their respective property abutts or is adjacent to, provided however that each of said owners shall be required to cut and remove said trees, timber and under- brush, only to the center of the street, avenue or alley upon which his property abutts or is adjacent to.

## Section 2.

That all of said work above ordered be completed by said owners within sixty days from the passage and approval and publication of this ordinance.

## Section 3.

That if any such promerty owner shall fail or refus e to cut and remove said trees, timber and underbrush from the portions of said streets, avenues or alleys upon which his particular property abutts or is adjacent to, within the time above provided, without reasonable excuse therefor, the Street Commissioner of the City of St. Maries, or any other person who may be authorized by the City Council, shall cause said trees, timber and underbrush to be cut and removed.

## Section 4.

Whenever said Street Commissioner, or other author- ized person, shall have caused any portion of said streets, avenue s or alleys to be cleared as afore said, he shall report that fact to the City Council at their next regular meeting, together with the name of the owner of the proerty abutting on or adjacent to the portion of said street, avenue or alley so cleared, together with a statement of the total cost of clearing same.

## Section 5.

The City Treasurer shall thereupon assess the cost thereof against the owner of such property and in the event that such owner shall fail to pay same within thirty days after receiv- ing notice thereof from said City Treasurer, the same may be re- covered from said owner, together with the costs of the proceedings by an action for such purpose before any Court of competent jurisdiction, brought in the corporate name of the City of St. Maries.

## Section 6.

The City Clerk is hereby ordered to post copies of this ordinance in at least five conspicuous places in said Govern- ment Townsite and to publish same in one issue of the official newspaper of the City of St. Maries, and such posting and publica- tion shall be deemed notice to all owners of such property.

## Section 7.

That this ordinance shall take effect and be in full force from and after its passage, approval and publication.

Passed by the City Council at a regular meeting thereof on the Fifth day of January 1914.

Approved this 5th day of January, A. D., 1914.

Mayor.

ATTEST:

Acting City Clerk.

Published in The Record Jan. 13, 1914.
