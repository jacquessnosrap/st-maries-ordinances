AN ORDINANCE vacating portions of Seventh Street, Idaho Avenue and the alley in Block Ten (10) of Railroad Addition, all in the City of St. Maries Benewah County, Idaho.

WHEREAS, a petition was, to-wit: on the 29th day of June, A. D 1916, filed with the Clerk of the City of St. Maries for the vacate ion of those portions of Seventh Street, Idaho, Avenue, and the alley in Block Ten (10) of Railroad Addition, hereinafter particularly des- cribed, and 

WHEREAS, THE Mayor and City Council of said City, by resolution duly adopted, ordered that said petition should be heard before said Coundl at a meeting thereof, to be held in the City Hall in said City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, on the 17th day of July, A. D. 1916, beginning at the hour of eight o'clock P.M., or as soon thereafter as said matter could be heard, and 

WHEREAS, it appears to the satisfaction of the Mayor and City Council of said City that more than seven days notice of the pendency of such application has been given by the written notices posted in five public places in said City, three of which notices were so posted in conspicuous places on or adjacent to the portions of said Street, Avenue, and alley sought to be vacated, and by causing such notice to be published not less than one week prior to the time fixed for the hearing of said application, in a newspaper published in the City of St. Maries, which said notices each contained a statement that a petition had been filed to vacate the portions of said Street, Avenue, and alley described in said petition (being the same portions of said Street, Avenue and alley hereinafter described) and contained a statement of the time and place fixed for the hearing of said petition, and 

WHEREAS, it appears that it is for the interests of said City of St. Maries and of the residents thereof that the prayer of said petition she be granteds 

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries:

## Section 1

That all of those portions of Seventh Street, Idaho Avenue, and the alley in Block Ten (10) of Railroad Addition to St. Maries, all in the City of St. Maries, Idaho, particularly described as follows, to-wit: (a) All that portion of Seventh Street in Railroad Addition to St. Maries lying southerly of the southerly line of St. Maries Avenue and northerly of the right of way and station grounds of the Chicago, Milwaukee and St. Paul Railway Company. (b) All that portion of Idaho Avenue in Railroad Addition to St. Maries lying westerly of the west line of Sixth Street, northerly of Block Eighteen, and southerly of Block Ten in Railroad Addition to St. Maries, and northerly of the right of way and station grounds of the Chicago, Milwaukee and St. Paul Railway Company: (c) Also, all of the alley in Block Ten (10) of Railroad Addit- ion to St. Maries, Idaho: be and the same are hereby, VACATED.

## Section 2

This ordinance shall take effect and be in force from and after its passage, approval and publication in one issue of the St. Maries Gazette, a weekly newspaper published in the City of St. Maries Idaho.
