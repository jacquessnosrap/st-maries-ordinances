An ordinance providing that certain male inhabitants of the road district of the Village of St. Maries, shall perform two days labor annually to be known as the road poll tax upon the streets, alleys, bridges, and highways within said road district, or pay a commutation fee of not more than four dollars to be fixed annually by the board of trustees of the Village of St. Maries; 

fixing the commutation fee for the road poll tax within said road district for the ensuing fiscal year;

defining the powers and duties of the road overseer of said road district providing for his salary; 

and repealing Ordinance No. 24 entitled "An ordinance providing that residents perform labor on the streets, alleys and highways, within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries, and fixing penalty in case of failure to appear".

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:

## Section 1

Every male inhabitant of the road district of the Village of St. Maries over the age of twenty one and under fifty years of age, shall perform two days labor annually to be known as the road poll tax, upon the streets, alleys, bridges and highways within said road district, under the demand and direction of the road overseer thereof, or pay to such overseer a commutation fee of four dollars or such smaller amount as may be fixed as the commutation fee by the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries.

## Section 2.

That the commutation fee for the road poll tax within the road district of the Village of St. Maries for the fiscal year ending on the Monday immediately preceding the first Tuesday of May, A.D. 1911, be, and the same hereby is fixed at the sum of four Dollars.

## Section 3.

The road overseer of the road district of the Village of St. Maries, shall have, within said road district, the powers conferred, and must perform the duties imposed, upon road overseers by Chapter 2, of Title 7 of Volume 1 of Idaho Revised Codes.

## Section 4.

That the road overseer of the road district of the Village of St. Maries, shall receive for his services, from money in the Treasurer s hand belonging to the road fund, the sum of not less than two dollars and fifty cents, and not more than four dollars, for each days service performed by him, to be fixed by the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, annually, at the time said board fixes the amount of the commutation fee for the road poll tax within the road district of the Village of St. Maries, to be audited and ordered paid by said board of trustees.

## Section 5.

That Ordinance No. 24 entitled An Ordinance providing that certain residence perform labor on the streets, alleys, and highways, within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries and fixing penalty in case of failure to appear, be, and the same hereby is, repealed.

## Section 6.

This Ordinance shall take effect after its passage, approval and publication.

Passed by the Board of Trustees this 28th, day of May, A. D. 1910. Approved by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees this 28th, day May A.D. 1910.

Attest: William Village Clerk. Chairman.
