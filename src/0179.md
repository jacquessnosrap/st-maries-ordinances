AN ORDINANCE CREATING A DISTRICT WITHIN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, BENEWAH COUNTY, IDAHO, IN WHICH NO BUILDING OR ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING SHALL BE CONSTRUCTED EXCEPT OF BRICK, CONCRETE OR OTHER INCOMBUSTIBLE MATERIAL, WITH FIRE PROOF ROOF, UNLESS THE SAME BE CONSTRUCTED ACCORDING TO CERTAIN SPECIFICATIONS HEREIN SET FORTH AND OTHER SPECIFICATIONS APPROVED BY THE BUILDING COMMITTEE OF THE CITY COUNCIL;

PROVIDING FOR THE APPOINTMENT OF A BUILDING COMMITTEE OF THE CITY COUNCIL AND DEFINING THE POWERS AND DUTIES OF SUCH COMMITTEE;

PROVIDING THE MANNER OF PROCEDURE IN SECURING THE APPROVAL OF ANY PLANS AND SPECIFICATIONS FOR BUILDINGS TO BE ERECTED WITHIN SAID DISTRICT;

DECLARING ANY BUILDING OR ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING HEREAFTER CONSTRUCTED WITHIN SAID DISTRICT A NUISANCE UNLESS THE SAME CONFORMS TO THE TERMS OF THIS ORDINANCE;

PROVIDING THAT THE CITY COUNCIL MAY REMOVE OR DEMOLISH OR CAUSE TO BE REMOVED OR DEMOLISHED ANY BUILDING OR ANY ADDITION TO ANY BUILDING HEREINAFTER ERECTED OR CONSTRUCTED IN SAID DISTRICT IN VIOLATION OF THIS ORDINANCE AND RECOVER THE COST THEREOF FROM THE OWNER OF SUCH BUILDING, AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT WITH THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ORDINANCE;

AND FIXING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION THEREOF.

Be it ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:-

## Section 1

That there be and there hereby is created a district within the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, within which no building or additions to buildings shall hereafter be constructed or erected except with brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material, with fire proof roof, unless the same be built in accordance with the provisions of this ordinance and in accordance with plans and specifications approved by the Building Committee of the City Council.

## Section 2

That said district shall include all the territory within said City included within the following boundaries, to-wit: On the South, the North line of College Avenue; on the West, the East line of Eleventh Street; on the North, the South line of the right of way of the Chicago, Milwaukee & St. Paul Railway down to its point of intersection with the North line of Main Avenue, and from there on East, the North boundary line of the City of St. Maries; and on the East the West boundary line of First Street.

## Section 3

That from and after the passage, approval and publication of this ordinance it shall be unlawful for any person or persons, corporation or corporations to erect or construct any building or addition to any building, within the above described district, except such building or addition as the case may be, be constructed of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material, with fire proof roof, or be constructed in the manner hereinafter provided.

## Section 4

It shall, however, be lawful for any person or persons, corporation or corporations to erect or construct a building within the above described district, of other material than of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material, with fire proof roof, provided such building be a plastered building, not over two stories in height, with a front footage of twenty-five feet or more, with a chimney of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material and cost not less than One Thousand ($1,000) Dollars, and PROVIDED FURTHER that such person, firm or corporation erecting such building have the written approval and permission of the Building Committee of the City Council to erect such building.

It shall also be lawful for any person or persons, corporation or corporations, to erect or construct an addition to any building, within the above described district of other material than of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material, provided that such person, firm or corporation erecting such addition to any building secure the written approval and permission of the Building Committee of the City Council to erect such building.

## Section 5

Any person or persons, corporation or corporations desiring to erect or construct any building or addition to any building within the said district other than of brick, stone, concrete or other incombustible material with fire proof roof, shall file with the City Clerk of the City of St. Maries his or its application in writing, stating the location of such proposed building, the probable use to which it will be put, and further stating that it is the intention to construct said building in accordance with the provisions and specifications of the foregoing section. Such application shall be accompanied by plans and specifications for such proposed building or addition to building, as the case may be, in sufficient detail to show the character and manner of construction of such building; and no application for any such building or addition to a building shall be considered or acted upon except the same be in writing, be filed with the City Clerk, and be accompanied by such plans and specifications in detail. At the time of filing such application with the City Clerk, there shall also be paid to such City Clerk for the use of the City of St. Maries a fee of $3.50 to cover the expenses of the City in connection with the publishing of the notice hereinafter provided for, and such other incidental expenses as it may be necessary to incur.

## Section 6

Upon the filing of such application and plans and specifications with him, the City Clerk shall immediately notify the Chairman of the Building Committee of the City Council of such filing, and the Chairman of such Committee shall forthwith fix the time and place of the hearing on such petition, and report to the City Clerk that such time and place has been fixed. Upon receiving such report the City Clerk shall cause to be published in the next issue of the official city newspaper a notice stating that said application has been filed, giving substantially the details of such application, and further stating the time and place when said building committee will consider the same. Such notice shall be published in one issue of said newspaper, and the time of the hearing on said application shall be not less than five (5) days after the first date of publication of the newspaper in which said notice appears.

## Section 7

At the time and place appointed, the said Building Committee shall meet and consider such application, and hear any and all objections thereto, and shall thereupon approve or disapprove such application and the accompanying plans and specifications, and return the same to the City Clerk with their action indorsed thereon. Upon the return of such application and plans and specifications, if the same be allowed and approved, the City Clerk shall issue to the person seeking permission for the erection of such building or addition, a permit for the erection of such building or addition, which permit shall be counter-signed by the Chairman of said Building Committee, and shall specify the place of construction of such building, the minimum cost thereof, and shall further specify that said permit is for the construction and erection of a building or an addition to a building, as the case may be, in accordance with this ordinance, and in accordance with the application, and the plans and specifications therefor, as allowed and approved by the Building Committee, and not otherwise. If the said application and plans and specifications be not allowed and approved by said Building Committee, then no further action thereon shall be had. In either case of approval or disapproval, the action of said Building Committee shall be final and binding.

## Section 8

That no building or addition to any building shall be moved into the district hereby created, or be moved from place to place therein, hereafter, except the same be of brick, stone, concrete or other combustible material, with fire-proof roof, without the written permission of the Building Committee. That such permission of the Building Committee for such moving shall be secured by the same procedure as is herein provided for the securing of a permission to construct or erect a building of combustible material, the necessary changes and substitutions being made, and Provided, that plans and specifications need not be filed with the application unless construction work or improvements are contemplated on the building moved.

## Section 9

That after the passage, approval, and publication of this ordinance, the Mayor of the City of St. Maries be and he hereby is directed and authorized and empowered to appoint from the members of the City Council a Building Committee of three members, and to designate the chairman thereof, which committee shall have the powers and duties conferred upon them by this ordinance, and shall have full jurisdiction and authority in the premises. Such committee shall be a standing committee of the City Council.

## Section 10

That any building or addition to any building of combustible material hereafter erected, constructed, or moved within or into said district, except the same be done in accordance with the provisions of this ordinance, shall be and the same hereby is declared to be a nuisance and the City Council may remove or demolish the same, or cause to be removed or demolished any such building or addition to building erected, constructed, or moved within or into said district, of combustible material, in violation of this ordinance, and the costs thereof may be recovered from the owner of such building or addition by an action in the Police Court of said City, or in any other Court of competent jurisdiction.

## Section 11

Any person or persons, firm or corporation violating or causing to be violated any of the terms of this ordinance, shall in addition to the penalty imposed on the property in Section 10, be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction thereof shall be punished by a fine of not less than $25.00 nor more than $100.00, and in case of default in the payment of such fine may be confined in prison in lieu thereof at the rate of one day for every $2.00 of such fine.

## Section 12

All ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herewith are hereby repealed and in particular is Ordinance No. 155 of the City of St. Maries hereby repealed.

Passed its first reading at an adjourned meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, held on the 13th day of December 1915.

Passed its second reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, at a regular meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho held on the 3rd day of 1915.

Passed its third reading, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, under a suspension of the rules, upon which a yea and nay vote was taken, at a regular meeting of the City Council of the City of St. Maries held the 7th day of February 1916.

Approved this 7th day of February 1916.

attest with corporate seal:-
Jos. R. Schulte City Clerk.
