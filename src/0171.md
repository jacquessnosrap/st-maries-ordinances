STATING THAT THE COST AND EXPENSE OF SAID PROPOSED STREET IMPROVEMENTS IS TO BE ASSESSED AGAINST ALL THE PROPERTY ABUTTING, FRONTING, CON- TIGUOUS OR TRIBUTARY TO (AND INCLUDED IN THE ASSESSMENT DISTRICT TO BE PROVIDED) THE SAID PORTIONS OF SAID DESCRIBED STREETS AN D AVENUES HEREIN PROPOSED TO BE IMPROVED; AND

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:-

## Section 1

That it is the intention of the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho, to improve that portion of Coeur d'Alene Street in the City of St. Maries lying between the South line of Main Avenue as projected West- ward and the North line of Main Avenue as projected Westward, and that portion of Coeur d'Alene Street lying between the North line of Main Avenue as projected Westward and the South line of Fifteenth Street (which is also a portion of the West line of Coeur d'Alene Street); and that portion of Fifteenth Street from the West line of Coeur de Alene Street to the North line of Center Avenue; and that portion of Center Avenue from the West line of Fifteenth Street to the East line of Twenty-third Street, by grading said portions of said streets and avenues and draining the same by a drainage ditch along and in front of blocks Forty and Forty-one, Government Townsite to the City of St. Maries and by making whatever other incidental improvements that may be necessary to permit of said grading.

That all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict herein are hereby repealed.
