An ordinance making it lawful to operate and keep open theatres and moving picture shows within the corporate limits of the village of St. Maries on Sundays; 

providing regulations and restrictions therefor, and providing a penalty for violations of certain provisions thereof.

Whereas a number of the qualified electors residing within the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, equal to a majority of the votes cast at the last general election. of said Village have petitioned this board of trustees of the Village of St. Maries that theatres and moving picture shows be allowed to operate and keep open within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries, on Sundays:

Whereas such petition has been accepted and approved by the Board of Trustees of the Viliage of St. Maries:

Now therefore be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho:

## Section 1

That it be. and hereby is made lawful for such theatres as are reg- ularly conducted within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries, Idaho, and are operated and kept open for the entertaininent of the public at certain and established hours, to keep open and operate on Sundays moving pictures and present on such days shows, operas and performances; provided however, that no moving pictures may be shown on Sundays which depict immoral or inde cent scenes, prize-fights, cock-fights or sparring matches, or which refer in a contemning or derogatory manner or way to the Deity; provided further, that no show, opera, spectacle or periormance shall be presented on a Sunday which contains or embraces a dancing act, sparring match, prize-fight or any im- moral or indecent exhibition.

## Section 2

Any person, persons or corporation, operating or conducting a moving picture show or theatre of the kind provided for and mentioned in Section 1 of this ordinance who shall show on a Sunday moving pictures which depict scenes which are immoral or in- decent in character, or which depict prize-fights, cock-fights, sparring match- es, or which contain a reference in a contemning or derogatory manner to the Deity, or any such person, persons or corporation presenting a show, or opera, spectacle or performance on a Sunday which contains or embraces a dancing act, sparring match, prize-fight or any immoral or indecent exhibition shall be subject to a fine of not more than $50.00 and not less than $25.00 and such person or corporation shall not be allowed to operate or conduct any moving picture show or theatre within the corporate limits of the Village of St. Maries within a period of three months after being so fined.

## Section 3

This ordinance shall be ef- fective aiter its passage, approval and publication.

Passed by the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries the 25th day of August, A. D. 1911.

Approved by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees the 25th day of Aug- ust, A. D. 1911.
