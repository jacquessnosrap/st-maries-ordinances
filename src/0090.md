An ordinance authorizing and directing the levying and collection of a new assessment or re-assessment to pay the cost and expense of the sewer system specified and provided for in Ordinance No. 76, and providing for the payment therefore.

Whereas, pursuant to the provisions of Ordinance No. 76 of the Village of St. Maries, an assessment roll has been heretofore prepared and filed. assessing the cost and expense of the sewer system specified in said ordinance, or a portion thereof, against the lots and parcels of lands included within the boundary lines of Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 1, of the Village of St. Maries, as the same is established by said ordinance and

Whereas, by reason of certain alleged informalities and irregularities in the proceedings had in the establishing of the said improvement district and in the levying of the said assessment, question has been raised as to the validity of the said assessment; and 

Whereas, there is a deficit in teh agregate amount heretofore assessed against the said lots and parcels of lands, and it is necessary to raise the sum of Two Thousand ($2,000) Dollars, over and above the amount heretofore assessed against the said lots and parcels of lands, in order to pay the full cost and expense of the said sewer system,, all whereof should be raised by special assessments against the lots and parcels of lands specially benefitted;

Therefore, Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries:
