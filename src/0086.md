AN ORDINANCE DECLARING THE INTENTION OF THE BOARD OF TRUSTEES OF THE VILLAGE OF ST. MARIES TO IMPROVE THAT PORTION OF COLLEGE AVENUE LYING AND BEING BETWEEN THE EAST LINE OF FIRST STREET AND THE WEST LINE OF FOURTEENTH STREET; STATING THE GENERAL CHARACTER OF THE PROPOSED IMPROVEMENT AND THE ESTIMATE OF THE COST OF THE SAME; STATING THAT THE COST OF THE PROPOSED IMPROVEMENT IS TO BE ASSESSED AGAINST THE PROPERTY ABUTTING ON SAID AVENUE BETWEEN SAID EAST AND WEST LINES AND INCLUDED IN THE LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT THEREIN PROPOSED; FIXING THE TIME IN WHICH PROTESTS AGAINST SAID PROPOSED IMPROVEMENT MAY BE FILED IN THE OFFICE OF THE VILLAGE CLERK; PROPOSING THE CREATION OF A LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT TO BE KNOWN AS "LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NO. 1." AND PROVIDING THAT SUCH LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT SHALL INCLUDE CERTAIN PROPERTY THEREIN DESCRIBED; AND PROVIDING THAT, ON SAID AVENUE AND BETWEEN SAID EAST AND WEST LINES, THE VILLAGE OF ST. MARIES SHALL PAY THE EXPENSE OF ALL IMPROVEMENTS IN THE SPACE FORMED BY THE JUNCTION OF TWO OR MORE STREETS, AND THE EXPENSE OF STREET CROSSINGS, CROSS-WAYS, ETC. Be it Ordained by the Chairman and the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai County, State of Idaho:

## Section 1
That the Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries declare, and it hereby does declare, its intention to improve that portion of College avenue lying and being between the east line of First street and the west line of Fourteenth street, by grading the same according to the official grade and elevation thereof as fixed and established by Section 4 of Ordinance No. 83.

## Section 2
The estimated cost of said proposed improvement is dollars, and the cost of said proposed improvement is to be assessed against the property abutting upon said College avenue between said east and west lines, and extending north and south to the center of the blocks fronting and abutting upon said avenue between said east and west lines, and to be included in the improvement district hereinafter proposed in section 4 hereof.

## Section 3
Protests against said proposed improvement may be filed in the office of J. E. Williams, Clerk of said Village of St. Maries at any time before 4 o'clock P.M. on the 18th day of July A.D. 1910.

## Section 4
That the property to be assessed for the cost of said proposed improvement shall be included in an assessment district hereafter created and to be known as "Local Improvement District No. 1," which said local improvement district shall include all the property fronting and abutting upon said College avenue between the east line of First street and the west line of Fourteenth street, to a distance back from such avenue to the center of the blocks fronting and abutting upon said avenue between said east and west lines.

## Section 5
That, between said east and west lines on said avenue, the expense of all improvements in the space formed by the junction of two or more streets, or where one main street terminates in or crosses another main street, and also all necessary street crossings or cross-ways at the corners, and the intersections of streets shall be paid by the Village of St. Maries.

## Section 6
This ordinance shall take effect after its passage, approval and publication.

Passed by the Board of Trustees, at a special meeting called for that purpose, this 1st day of July A.D. 1910.

Approved by the Chairman of the Board of Trustees this 1st day of July A.D. 1910.
