An Ordinance Confirming and Approving the Creation and Establishment of Local Improvement District No. 4, in the City of St. Maries, Idaho, 

Confirming and Approving the Assessment Roll with Law;

Approving and Confirming all Proceedings had in Connection with the said Assessment Roll;

Providing for the Payment by Installments of the Cost and Expense of the Improvements made in said District;

Providing for the Issuance of Improvement Bonds of the District;

Assessing the cost of said Improvement Against the Lots, Blocks and Parcels of Land in said District Contiguous to, Fronting or Abutting on said Improvements, and Describing the Manner of Collection and Payment of said Assessments.

WHEREAS, the City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho, has heretofore, pursuant to the requisite preliminary proceedings and ordinance No. 176 of said City, established Local Improvement District No. 4 of said City, for the purpose of improving certain streets and avenues of said city, by grad- ing them, and draining the same by a drainage ditch along and in front of Blocks 40 and 41, Government Townsite Addition of the City of St. Maries, and making such other incidental improve- ments as may be necessary to permit of said grading; and

WHEREAS, the Committee on Streets and Alleys, together with the City Engineer, have heretofore, in strict comformity with the law and the ordinances of said city, made out an assess- ment roll in and for said Local Improvement District No. 4, fix- ing the total cost of the improvements therein taxable against private property at $5.630.000s, assessing said amount against the lots, blocks and parcels of land contiguous to, front- ing or abutting upon said improvements in said district. Said assessments being distributed in strict accordance with law, which assessment roll was filed with the City Clerk on the 131 day of ember A. D., 1916; and

WHEREAS, said assessment roll was properly certified by the City Council in strict accordance with law; and

WHEREAS, after receiving said assessment roll, the City Clerk under the direction of the City Council gave notice by three successive publications in the official newspaper the City of St. Maries, that such assessment roll was on file per of in his office, giving the date of filing the same, and stating the time at which the City Council would hear and consider objections to said assessment roll by the parties aggrieved by said assessment, to-wit: on the 100 day of October, 1916, and stating that the owner or owners of any property which was assessed in such assessment roll, whether named or not in such roll, might, within ten days after the last publication of said notice, file with the City Clerk his or her objections in writing to the regularity of the proceedings in making such assessment and the correctness of such assessment or of the amount levied on any property, lot or parcel of land; and

WHEREAS, there were no objections filed with the City Clerk or presented to the city Council agadust theasceinnsat levied by said assessment roll, and

WHEREAS, it is the opinion of the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, that the cost and expense of the improvements made in Local Improvement District No. 4, which are chargeable to the abutting, adjoining, contiguous or proximate property should be provided for and paid for by in- stallments as specified in the ordinance creating said Dist- rict No. 4; and

WHEREAS, the Mayor and City Council of the said City City desire to issue, in the name of the City, improvement bonds of the said district, to bear date the first day of January A. D. 1916, payable on or before ten years.

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT ORDAINED, by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That all of the proceedings, matters and things heretofore had, taken and done, in connection with the establishment of Local Improvement District No. 4, in the City of St. Maries, Idaho, and the making and constructing therein of the improvements consisting of the improving of certain streets and avenues, and parts of streets and avenues in said Improvement District, by grading them and draining the same by a drainage ditch along and in front of Blocks 40 and 41, Govern- ment Townsite Addition to the City of St. Maries, and making such other incidental improvements particularly specified in Ordinance No. 176, and the apportionment of the cost of said improvements against the lots, blocks, and parcels of land in said District, be, and the same hereby are in all respects ap- proved, ratified and confirmed.
