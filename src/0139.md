An Ordinance Amending Section Seven of Ordinance Number One Hundred and Thirty-two Entitled: An Ordinance Declaring and Fixing Salaries of the Officers of the City St. Maries, Idaho" by Declaring Fixing the Salaries of the Police Officers of the City of St. Maries.

Be it Ordained by the Mayor and Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That Section Seven (7) Ordinance Number One Hundred Thirty-two (132) be amended to read as follows, to-wit:

Seg. 7. That the salary of the police officers of the City of St. Maries eball be as follows, to-wit: Chief of Police, one hundred dollars ($100.00) per month All policemen and officers under the Chief, eighty-five dollars ($85.00) per month.

## Section 2

That all ordinances and parte of ordinances in conflict here- with are hereby repealed.

## Section 3

That this ordinance shall be in effect from and after its passage, approval, and publication in The Record, of St. Maries, Idaho.
Passed the City Council of the City of St. Maries this 2nd day of June, A. D. 1913.
Approved by the Mayor of the City of St. Maries this 2nd day of June, A. D. 1913.
HENRY L. MULCAHY, Mayor.
Attest: CHAS. R. SCHULTE, City Olerk
