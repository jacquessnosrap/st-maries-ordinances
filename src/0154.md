AN ORDINANCE PROVIDING FOR THE CALLING AND HOLDING OF A SPECIAL ELECTION IN AND FOR THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, KOOTENAI COUNTY, IDAHO, ON TUESDAY, THE 28TH DAY OF JULY, A. D., 1914,

FOR THE PURPOSE OF VOTING ON THE PROPOSITION OF ISSUING THE NEGOTIABLE MUNICIPAL COUPON BONDS OF SAID CITY TO AN AMOUNT AGGREGATING THE PRINCIPAL SUM OF TEN THOUSAND (10,000) DOLLARS,

FOR THE PURPOSE OF SALE, 

AND TO APPLY THE PROCEEDS THEREON TO THE IMPROVING OF THE INTERSECTIONS OF CERTAIN STREETS AND AVENUES WITHIN THE CITY OF ST. MARIES WHICH SAID INTERSECTIONS ARE CONTAINED WITHIN LOCAL IMPROVEMENT DISTRICT NO. 2 OF SAID CITY; 

PROVIDING HOW, AND IN WHAT MANNER SUCH BONDS SHALL BE ISSUED;

AND PROVIDING FOR AND DESIGNATING THE PROVISIONS TO BE MADE FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE PRINCIPAL AND INTEREST ON SUCH BONDS.

Whereas, there has been created within the City of St. Maries, "Local Improvement District No. 2" for the purpose of improving the streets and avenues included therein by grading them, gravelling them, curbing them with concrete curbing, putting in concrete gutters and the necessary culverts where necessary to permit of said grading, and raising or lowering the sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading, lowering the water pipes where necessary to permit of said grading, putting in retaining walls where necessary to protect said grading, and by moving such telephone and electric light poles as it may be necessary to move to permit of said grading,

And whereas, in Ordinance No. 150 of the City of St. Maries, which is an ordinance declaring the intention of the City Council of St. Maries to create said Local Improvement District No. 2, and in Ordinance No. 152, which is an ordinance creating said Local Improvement District No. 2, it is provided that the expense of the improvements in the spaces formed by the junction of two or more streets, or where any one street terminates in or crosses another street, and also of all necessary street crossings or cross walks at the intersections of said streets, and the expense of raising or lowering the water pipes and sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading, shall be paid by the City of St. Maries,

And whereas it is estimated that the cost of improving the spaces formed by the junction of two or more streets or where any one street terminates in or crosses another street, in Local Improvement District No. 2, by grading them, gravelling them, and otherwise improving them according to the provisions of Ordinances No. 150 and 152, and the expense of putting in the necessary street crossings at the corners and intersections of said streets contained in Local Improvement District No. 2, and the expense of raising or lowering the water pipes and sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading, in said Local Improvement District No. 2, will be altogether approximately Ten Thousand ($10,000.) Dollars.

And whereas there are no funds in the treasury of said City of St. Maries available for the purpose of improving said street intersections and doing the work above set forth, and whereas large benefits will accrue to the general public and to the citizens of said City by reason of the making of all of said improvements, and whereas it is deemed advisable and hereby declared necessary that the funds required for said purpose shall be raised by the issuance and sale of municipal improvement coupon bonds of said City to an amount aggregating the principal sum of Ten Thousand ($10,000.) Dollars,

And whereas the assessed full cash valuation of the real estate and personal property in said City of St. Maries according to the assessment of the preceding year 1913 as returned by the assessor is $675,852.00,

And whereas the total direct indebtedness of said City as evidenced by municipal coupon bonds, amounts to the sum of Seventeen Thousand (17,000.) Dollars, and is represented by that amount of five (5) per cent municipal improvement bonds bearing date of July 1, 1909 for the purpose and the proposed Ten Thousand ($10,000.) Dollar bond issue aforesaid, if authorized and issued in the manner hereinafter referred to, will, with said outstanding bonded indebtedness, aggregate Twenty-seven Thousand ($27,000.) Dollars.

And whereas said aggregate sum of Twenty-seven Thousand ($27,000.) Dollars does not exceed six (6) per cent of the assessed full cash valuation of the real estate and personal property of said City, according to the assessment of the preceding year 1913; Now Therefore, in consideration of the premises,

Be it ordained by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, as follows:

## Section 1

That a special election in and for the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, be and the same is hereby called to be held in the City of St. Maries, at the usual polling places in said city, to-wit: - at Shelton & Warren's Hall for the First Ward of said City, at the City Hall of the City of St. Maries for the Second Ward of said City, and at Nelson & Osmer's store building for the Third Ward of said City, on Tuesday, to-wit, the 28th day of July, A. D., 1914, between the hours of eight (8) o'clock A. M. and seven (7) o'clock, P. M. at which time there shall be submitted to the qualified electors of the City of St. Maries, who are tax-payers of said City, the proposition following for their vote and determination thereat, to-wit: "Shall the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Kootenai County, Idaho, be authorized to issue municipal improvement coupon bonds in the amount of Ten Thousand ($10,000.) Dollars; said bonds to bear interest semi-annually at a rate not exceeding six (6) per cent per annum, and the principal of said bonds to be paid at the option of the City of St. Maries, at any time after ten (10) years and must be paid within twenty (20) years from the time they are issued; said bonds being for the purpose of paying for the expenses of improving the spaces formed by the junction of two or more streets or where any one street terminates in or crosses another in Local Improvement District No. 2 of the City of St. Maries, by grading them, gravelling them and otherwise improving them in accordance with the provisions of Ordinances No. 150 and 152 of said City, and also the expense of putting in the necessary street crossings at the corners and intersections of the streets contained in said Local Improvement District No. 2 and the expense of raising or lowering the water pipes and sewer manholes where necessary to permit of said grading within said local improvement district."
