AN ORDINANCE PRESCRIBING CERTAIN RULES AND REGULATIONS FOR THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE WATER DEPARTMENT OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES;

PROVIDING THAT ALL CONSUMERS OF WATER WITHIN SAID CITY OF ST. MARIES SHALL BE FURNISHED WATER ON A METER BASIS;

PROVIDING FOR THE INSTALLATION OF SAID METERS AND FOR THE PAYMENT OF THE COST OF SAID METERS AND SAID INSTALLATION THEREOF;

PROVIDING THE RATES TO BE CHARGED TO WATER CONSULERS BY THE WATER DEPARTMENT OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES FOR THE USE OF WATER;

PROHIBITING INTERFERENCE WITH OR INJURY TO ALL PARTS AND APPLIANCES OF THE MUNICIPAL WATER WORKS OF THE CITY OF ST. MARTES;

PRESCRIBING A PENALTY FOR THE VIOLATION OF ANY OF THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ORDINANCE AND REPEALING ALL ORDINANCES AND PARTS OF ORDINANCES IN CONFLICT HEREWITH.

BE IT ORDAINED by the Mayor and City Council of the City of St. Maries, Idaho:

## Section 1

That immediately upon the taking effect of this Ordinance, all water consumers in the City of St. Maries taking water from the water department of said City shall be placed upon a meter basis, and be required to install water meters in their premises, as a condition precedent to the use of purchase of water from the water department.

That immediately upon the taking effect of this Ordinance the water department shall proceed to install upon the premises of all water users, water meters, which meters shall be purchased by the City of St. Maries and installed at the expense of said City.

That the cost of said meters and of the installation thereof shall be chargeable to each of said property owners at the rate of Nine (9) dollars cash when installed, or at the rate of Ten (310) dollars each, payable on time at the rate of Fifty cents per month, until the entire sum of Ten dollars shall be paid.

That hereafter when application shall be made to the water department of the City of St. Maries for the use of water, the water department shall install a water meter on the premises of said applicant under the same conditions as aforesaid, and the cost of such meter and the cost of the installation thereof shall be the same as stated in the preceding paragraph.

When the water consumer pays for said meters on time at the price of Ten (10) dollars each, and at the rate of fifty cents rer month, such monthly installments of fifty cents each shall be added to the regular monthly water bill of such consumer, or in case there may be no water charge against such consumer for any particular month, such installment of fifty cents shall constitute such con- sumers' water charge for such month provided, however, that in the event that said monthly installment of fifty cents, or any part thereof shall remain unpaid on the 10th day of each and every month, the same will become delinquent, and if still delinquent on the 15th day of the month, the same penalty will attach to said water consumer and his premises, as shall attach in case of a non-payment of the water charge for such premises, and in addition thereto the water department of the City of St. Maries shall, have the right to enter upon said premises and remove said water meter therefrom, and all rayments made upon said water meter shall be forfeited and the said City shall have the right to retain the payments made, for and in consideration of the rent for such water meter during the time that the same was in use.

Such water meters so installed shall be and remain the pro- perty of the City of St. Maries until fully paid for by the purchaser, but upon the payment by such purchaser or water consumer of the full sum of Nine (39) dollars cash or of Ten ($10) dollars when paid on time, each water meter so paid for shall become the property of such water consumer or purchaser.
