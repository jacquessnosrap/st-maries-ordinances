AN ORDINANCE ENTITLED "THE ANNUAL APPROPRIATION BILL" FOR THE FISCAL YEAR, OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, COMMENCING ON THE FIRST TUESDAY IN MAY, 1916; 

APPROPRIATING THE SUM OF $25,000.00 TO DEFRAY THE NECESSARY EXPENSES AND LIABILITIES OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES, IDAHO, FOR THE SAID FISCAL YEAR: SPECIFYING THE OBJECT AND PURPOSES FOR WHICH SUCH APPROPRIATIONS ARE MADE, AND THE AMOUNT APPROPRIATED FOR EACH OBJECT OR PURPOSE;AND

LEVYING A TAX OF THIRTEEN MILLS ON EACH DOLLAR OF VALUATION ON ALL TAXABLE PROPERTY, REAL AND PERSONAL, WITHIN THE CORPORATE LIMITS OF THE CITY OF ST. MARIES.

BE IT ORDAINED by the mayor and city council of the City of St. Maries, Benewah County, Idaho:-

## Section 1

That the sum of $25,000.00 be, and the same is hereby appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources and from the tax levy hereinafter provided for, to de- fray the necessary expenses and liabilities of the City of St. Maries, for the fiscal year commencing on the first Tuesday in May 1916, and that said sum be appropriated for the purposes and objects in the amounts hereinafter provided.

## Section 2

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from all sources during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of 36,000.00 to be known as the Salary Fund, to be used for the rurpose of paying the salaries of the city officers and employees.

## Section 3

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from the thirteen mills tax levy, hereby provided and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $850.DO to be known and designated at the 1909 Water Bonds Sinking Fund, to be used for the purpose of redeeming all of the outstanding Bonded indebtedness of hess of said city upon its Water Works system as privided by Ordinance No. 70, of said city.

## Section 4

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from the said mill tax levied herein and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $850.00 to be known as the Water Works Interest Fund for the purpose of pay- ing the interest on the outstanding bonded indebtedness of said city, upon its water works system as provided in Ordinance No.. 70, of said city.

## Section 5

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from all sources of said city during said fiscal year, not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $2,000.00 to be known and designated as the Water Works Fund, to be used for the purpose of extending and maintaining the water workssystem of said city.

## Section 6

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from all sources of said city during said fiscal year and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $2,400.00 to be known as the Light Fund, to be used for the purpose of lighting the streets, avenues, alleys and public buildings of said city.

## Section 7

That there be and there hereby is appropriated out of all moneys received from all sources by said city during said fiscal year, and not otherwise appropriated; the sum of $1,000.00 to be known as the Fire Fund, to be used for fire protection purposes in said city.

## Section 8

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of the mill annual tax levy herein provided and not otherwise appropriated the sum of $300.00 to be known as the Library Fund, to be used for the purpose of maintaining a public Library in said city and paying the expenses thereof.

## Section 9

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from all sources by said city during said fiscal year and not otherwise appropriated, the sum. of $5,000.00 to be known as the Current Exrense Fund to be used for the purpose of de-fraying the current expense of said city during said year.

## Section 10

That there be and there is hereby appro- priated out of all moneys received from the annual tax levy here- in provided, and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $2,000.00 to be known as the Warrant Redemption Fund, to be used for the purpose of paying the outstanding warrant indebtedness of said city on account of warrants issued by said city and now outstand - ing.

## Section 11

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from all sources by said city during said fiscal year and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $3,000.00 to be known as the Public Improvement Fund to be used for the purpose of paying for public improvements in said city during said year.

## Section 13

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from said mill tax levied herein, and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $600.00 to be known as the Improvement District No. 2 Street Intersection Bond Interest Fund, for the purpose of paying the interest upon the outstanding bonded indebtedness of said city as privided in Ordinance No. 156 of said city.

## Section 14

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from said mill tax levied herein, and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $2,200.00 to be known as the 1915 Water Works Bonds Interest Fund for the purpose of paying the interest upon the outstanding bonded indebtedness, of said city upon its water works system and provided in ordinance No. 165 of said city.

## Section 15

That there be and there hereby is appro- priated out of all moneys received from said mill tax herein levied and not otherwise appropriated, the sum of $500.00 to be known as the Beaton Judgment Fund for the purpose of paying the interest and a portion of the principal of that certain judgment recovered against the city of St. Maries by Anna Beaton and John Beaton, her husband.

## Section 16

That there be and there hereby is levied upon all the taxable property, both real and personal, within the corporate limits of the City of St. Maries, subject to taxation for the fiscal year commencing on the first Tuesday in May, 1916, the sum of thirteen mills on each dollar of valuation.

## Section 17

That it shall be the duty of the City Treasurer to distribute the moneys received by said city during said year from said annual tax levy and from all other sources and appropriate the same to the various funds as herein provided for and to keep a separate account of the receipts credited to each fund and expended the refrom.

## Section 18

That all ordinances and parts of ordinances in conflict with this ordinance are hereby repealed.

## Section 19

That this ordinance shall take effect and be in force upon its passage, approval and publication.
