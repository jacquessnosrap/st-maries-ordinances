# Ordinance 20

An ordinance makign it unlawful for children under the age of sixteen years, to be on the streets, or other public places within the corporate limits of the village of St Maries, without authority, between certain hours and fixing the penalty for violation and defining the duties of officers in relation thereto.

Be it ordained by the chairman and board of trustiees of the village of St Maries Kootenai County Idaho

## Section 1
It shall be unlawful for childre under the age of sixteen year to be on the highways, bridges, streets, or alleys or other public places iwthin the corporate limits of the village of St Maries, between the hours of nine o clock in the evenign to the hour of five o clock in the mornign from May 1st to October 31st of each year and between the hourse of eight o clock in the evenign to the hour of five o clock the next morning from November 1st to April 30th of each year. Provided, however, that children under the above age, may be on saidl highways, bridges, streets, alleys, or other public places, during the specified time, when accompanied by their parents or guardian or when such children have a written permit from their parent or parents or guardian, for the purpose of going or comming from any errand as the carrying of any message, or either without such permit while going or coming from any gathering in a hall, church, school building or other house of entertainment or while going or coming from a visit to any house when a family or families are residing.

## Section 2
The penalty for the violation of section 1 of this ordinance shall be a fine of two dollars and costs, or imprisonment of one day in the village jail for every offence commited,

## Section 3
It shall be the duty of the village marshal and night watchman to enforce section 1 of this ordinance. Every child under the age of 16 years, found on any public place without the authority provided for in this ordinance during the hours designated, shall be ordered to its respective home or place of abode and on refusal or non-compliance with such order, shall be lodged in the village jail until the following morning, when it shall be brought before a justice of the peace and arranged on teh charge of violating section 1 of this ordinance.

## Section 4
This ordinance shall go into effect upon its approval and publication.

Approved May 11, 1903
