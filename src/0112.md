# Ordinance No. 112

> [!NOTE]
> This ordinance is in the process of being transcribed and has not been completely transcribed. Please see original document for complete text.

An ordinance creating Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 4 in the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai Cuunty, Idaho, defining the boundaries thereof and providing for the construction of a sewerage system therein;

providing for the appointment of a sewer committee and defining and regulating their powers and duties;

providing for the levying of a special assessment for the sewerage improvement and the expenses connected therewith and providing for the issuing of bonds therefor;

providing for the payment of said assessment in ten (10) equal annual installments and for the collection thereof;

prescribing the duties of the village clerk and village treasurer in connection with the collection thereof;

providing for the redemption of said bonds;

providing for the assessment of the property in said Local Sewerage Improvement District No. 4 and for the preparation of an assessment roll and for the hearing of protests against the same and providing for the appealing of all ordinances in conflict therewith.

Be it ordained by the Chairman and Board of Trustees of the Village of St. Maries, Kootenai county. Idaho;
